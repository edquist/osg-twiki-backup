Welcome to the home of <b>%WIKITOOLNAME%.%WEB%</b>. This is a web-based collaboration area for each of the OSG Virtual Organizations to provide its own VO-specific information to its members.  Create pages to record your VO-specific practices, configurations, etc. for users and admins.  Please organize the information according to the given links.  Record your experiences, your success/failure rate at each OSG site, and other information that your members and/or other VOs may find useful. <!-- In that ideal future, we will then provide an easy to navigate way for site admins to see their score, and other VOs to see which sites are worthwhile trying out, and how to go about it.-->

---++ Legend for Table
#VeeO
   $ VO: The VO name; target page should include overview of VO, OSG contacts for VO, link to VO's home page, intro to VO's implementation of OSG software, links to any news items.
#UtilStat
   $ Util Status: Utilization status for VO
#UserInfo
   $ User info: Target page should include/link to info such as how to join VO, what CA(s) to use, how to select resources, how to use application, and your VO's resource access policies
#AppL
   $ Application: Target page should include/link to all required information for application admins
#SuPport
   $ Support: Target page should include/link to VO-specific support information (phone numbers, procedures, etc.)
#PriVlg
   $ Privileges: Target page should include/link to VO's defnition of member groups and roles, and their associated privileges.
#SuccFail
   $ Success/Failure: Target page is intended for recording your VO's success/failure rate at each site, plus any comments, concerns, etc.
