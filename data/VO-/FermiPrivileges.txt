---+ Roles, Groups and Privileges in the fermilab VO

The top level groups are used to identify the experiments and projects that fall under the fermilab VO umbrella. For experiment- or project-specific group hierarchies and role definitions, see the experiment/project pages.  If you register in the [[https://voms.fnal.gov:8443/vomrs/vo-fermilab/vomrs][fermilab VOMRS]], you will see the group/role structure. 

For more information, contact _helpdesk-admin@fnal.gov_.
