---+ Success/Failure of fermilab VO test jobs on remote sites

The fermilab VO runs a probe to test sites. 
   * From the http://fermigrid.fnal.gov/ home page, click _FermiGrid Metrics and Service Monitors_.  
   * See the table labelled _FermiGrid - fermilab Virtual Organization probe_.
   * Look for _fermilab VO probe - Production Gridcat_. The associated links provide success/failure information in different formats and in differing levels of detail.
