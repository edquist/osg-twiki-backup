---+ OSGEDU VO
---++ OSGEDU Privilege Information
---+++ Introduction

Everyone that has completed one of our training workshops is encouraged to become an active part of the !OSGEDU community. The DOE certificate received during the workshop allows  usage of the !OSGEDU resources, and even more. If you haven't attended any of our training events, but you are interested in  joining the !OSGEDU VO, please send a message to *eot@opensciencegrid.org*. In addition, you can learn more about our *Education Program at [[http://www.opensciencegrid.org/Education][www.opensciencegrid.org/Education]]*.

%RED% [a document describing the process of joining the !OSGEDU VO in detail will be soon posted]. %ENDCOLOR%

This page describes the !OSGEDU VO privilege information needed to allow the services within the !OSGEDU VO access to a particular site. Included here is the GUMS and VOMS information that must be in place.

---+++ OSGEDU VO Usage Rules

The purpose of the !OSGEDU VO is to provide standard services to educational projects associated with the Open Science Grid.

   * !OSGEDU Usage Policy - !OSGEDU VO users agree to the !OSG Usage Policy located here.
   * Action Within the Scope of !OSG - Users will not use the !OSGEDU VO to run applications outside the scope of the educational efforts of the !OSG.
   * Incident Reporting - If a machine containing the credentials for a user is compromised the administator of that service agrees to contact the !OSG-GOC at goc@opensciencegrid.org
