---+ Security Team Email Lists

_The following addresses are open for use by all OSG members, partners, and collaborators:_

*security@opensciencegrid.org* is the standard email address open to the OSG public %RED%for reporting of security incidents%ENDCOLOR%. Mail to *security@opensciencegrid.org* is received by the OSG Grid Operations Center (GOC). It is monitored 24x7 and filtered for spam or other off-topic content.  The GOC acts immediately on all mail incoming to this address.

_The following addresses are for internal use by OSG staff:_

*osg-security-team@opensciencegrid.org* is for internal discussions among OSG security team members. 

See [[Documentation.ContactsMailingLists][Consortium Mailing Lists]] for a full listing of OSG mailing lists.
