---+ Wednesday Exercise 5.1: Running jobs in the OSG

The goal of this exercise is to submit jobs to HTCondor and have them run on opportunistic resources on the OSG.

---++ Submitting Jobs to the Local Cluster

   1. From yesterday's exercises, find and copy a submit file that queues many jobs and makes use of =$(Process)= and =$(Cluster)= in its =output= field.
   1. Change the number of jobs to queue to 10.
   1. Change the executable command so that it runs the =hostname= command.
   1. Submit the file and track your jobs through =condor_q=

---++ Submitting Jobs to the OSG

   1. Copy the submit file from the previous section.
   1. In the submit file, add the following two lines above the =queue= statement:\
<pre class="file">+WantGlidein = True
Requirements = (PoolName =!= 'CHTC')</pre>
   1. Submit the file and track your jobs through =condor_q=
