Getting your research applications onto the Open Science Grid (OSG) can be daunting to first time users. The OSG Education, Training and Outreach (OSG-ETO) activity provides researchers in every field with tools to shorten this learning curve and speed them onto using this powerful computational resource. In the following pages you will find information about the work of the OSG-EOT program and how individual researchers, research groups and educators can see clear benefits from the online tutorials, seminars and other grid-oriented education our progran provides. You will also find details on the upcoming Grid Workshops and Grid Schools, the application process and guidelines on how to access the online education materials.
