---++Short Description
The OSG Education Activity provides training for student users of the OSG and site administrators and is collaborating with other educational grid groups.

---++Training
Training is provided in organized around the world through the year Grid Workshops. 

Upcoming Grid Schools:
   * The ArgentinaGridWorkshop, March 4-9, 2007 in Argentina
   * The MidwestGridWorkshop, March 26-30, 2006 at University of Illinois in Chicago Illinois

Visit our GridSchoolsPage to view past Grid Workshops and the material introduced in these courses.

---++Collaborations
The OSG Education group is collaborating with other grid education groups around the world. 

---+++Collaboration with I2U2
OSG is collaborating with [[http://ed.fnal.gov/uueo/i2u2.html][&#8220;Interactions in Understanding The Universe&#8221; (I2U2)]] in the support of e-Labs based on Grid middleware, as well with the [[http://www.teragrid.org/][TeraGrid]] in its Education and Training activities.  

---+++Collaboration with Mariachi experiment
The [[http://cosmicray.bnl.gov][Mariachi experiment]] to detect extreme energy cosmic rays (EECRs) is also a member of OSG and participates in education and outreach activities.

---+++ Collaboration with Scandinavia to benefit Young Researchers
[[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Education/OISE][OSG's international outreach and education activities with Scandinavia]] has three activities which cover different groups of young researchers and groups with whom we are collaborating. 
   * [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Education/OISE#Interoperation_of_the_NorduGrid][Interoperation of the NorduGrid Distributed Facility and Open Science Grid]]
   * [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Education/OISE#Application_support_for_the_IceC][Application support for the IceCube collaboration]]
   * [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Education/OISE#Joint_Grid_Training][Joint Grid Training ]]
Please contact [[mailto:osg-eb@opensciencegrid.org][osg-eb@opensciencegrid.org]] if you are interested in any of these programs.
