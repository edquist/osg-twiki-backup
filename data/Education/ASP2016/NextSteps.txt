---++ What's next after the grid school ? ...

For people interested in continuing work on grid computing and OSG resources, please go to our online self-paced course:
[[http://opensciencegrid.org/OnlineGridCourse][opensciencegrid.org/OnlineGridCourse]]. Follow the steps -- you will be asked to sign up for an account on University of Chicago machines and also to apply for a DOE certificate. For easy, direct access to that page, you can go to the [[http://www.ci.uchicago.edu/osgedu/schools/gridlab/intro.html][new account and cert page]].
