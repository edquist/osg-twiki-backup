---++Workshop Syllabus

Each session consists of 1 hour of lecture and 2 hours of exercises.

---+++Monday March 12, 2007

---++++Morning: Introduction to Grids

*Slides:* [[%ATTACHURL%/1-overview.ppt][1-overview.ppt]]

*Exercises:* [[AGWIntroExercises][Getting Used to the Environment]]

---++++Afternoon: Security; Job and Resource management
*Topics:* Grid security infrastructure, authentication, authorization, VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo management, proxies, delegation, GRAM, Condor

*Slides:*  [[%ATTACHURL%/2a-security.ppt][2a-security.ppt]] [[%ATTACHURL%/2b-jobs.ppt][2b-jobs.ppt]]


*Exercises:* [[AGWSecurityExercises][Security]], [[AGWJobExercises][Job and Resource Management]]

*Related:* [[http://www.cs.wisc.edu/condor/description.html][What is Condor?]]; [[http://www.globus.org/toolkit/gram/][GRAM]]

---+++Tuesday March 13, 2007

---++++Morning: Data management; Grid information systems
*Topics:* !GridFTP, I/O performance issues, reliable file transfer service, Replication and replica catalog services, MDS, !GridCat

*Slides:* [[%ATTACHURL%/3a-data.ppt][3a-data.ppt]] [[%ATTACHURL%/3b-info.ppt][3b-info.ppt]]

*Exercises:* [[AGWDataExercises][Data Management]] [[AGWInfoExercises][Information and Monitoring]]

---++++Afternoon: Science Workflows on the Grid
*Topics:* Problems, case studies

*Slides:* [[%ATTACHURL%/4-workflow.ppt][4-workflow.ppt]]

*Exercises:* [[AGWWorkflowExercises][Managing Workflows]]. There are some example solutions from Damián Montaldo in [[%ATTACHURL%/example.tar][example.tar]]

*Related information:* [[http://www.cs.wisc.edu/condor/dagman/][DAGman]], [[http://www.ci.uchicago.edu/swift/][Swift]]

---+++Wednesday March 14, 2007 

---++++Morning: Building Grids
*Topics:* clusters; campus grids; national grids; international grids

*Slides:*  [[%ATTACHURL%/5-building-nat-grid-inf.ppt][5-building-nat-grid-inf.ppt]]

*Related information:* [[http://www.opensciencegrid.org/?pid=1000059][Getting started on the Open Science Grid]]


---++++Afternoon: Catch-up / open discussion
