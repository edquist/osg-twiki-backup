---++ Overview
The OSG Education Activity provides training for users and site administrators of the OSG.  It is collaborating with other educational grid groups. 

---++ Training
Training is provided in Grid Workshops held around the world, throughout the year. 

---+++ _Grid Schools:_
   * *[[FloridaInternationalGridSchool2008][Florida International Grid School]], Wednesday-Friday January 23-25, 2008, Florida International University, Miami, FL*
   * [[GreatPlainsGridSchool2007][Great Plains Grid School]], Wednesday-Friday August 8-10, 2007, University of Nebraska at Lincoln
   * [[RioGrandeGridSchool2007][Rio Grande Grid School]], Friday-Sunday June 8-10, 2007, The University of Texas at Brownsville
   * The [[MidwestGridWorkshop][Midwest Grid Workshop]], March 24-25, 2006 at University of Illinois at Chicago
   * The ArgentinaGridWorkshop, March 12-14, 2007 in [[http://escuelapav.ceride.gov.ar/index.php][Santa Fe, Argentina ]]
   * The TierThreeWorkshop, March 7, 2006 at University of California - San Diego (part of the OSG Consortium Meeting)

Visit our GridWorkshops to view information and material from Grid Workshops.

---++ Collaborations
The OSG Education group is collaborating with other grid education groups around the world:

---+++ _Collaboration with I2U2_
OSG is collaborating with [[http://ed.fnal.gov/uueo/i2u2.html][&#8220;Interactions in Understanding The Universe&#8221; (I2U2)]] in the support of e-Labs based on Grid/TeraGrid middleware. 

---+++ _Collaboration with !TeraGrid_
OSG is collaborating with [[http://www.teragrid.org/][TeraGrid]] in its Education and Training activities.  

---+++ _Collaboration with Mariachi_
OSG is collaborating with the [[http://cosmicray.bnl.gov][Mariachi experiment]] -- a member of OSG that is working to detect extreme energy cosmic rays (EECRs) -- on education and outreach activities.

---+++ _Collaboration with Scandinavia to benefit Young Researchers_
[[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Education/OISE][OSG's international outreach and education activities with Scandinavia]] include three education activities addressed to different groups of young researchers and groups that collaborate with OSG: 
   * [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Education/OISE#Interoperation_of_the_NorduGrid][Interoperation of the NorduGrid Distributed Facility and Open Science Grid]]
   * [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Education/OISE#Application_support_for_the_IceC][Application support for the IceCube collaboration]]
   * [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Education/OISE#Joint_Grid_Training][Joint Grid Training ]]
Please contact [[mailto:osg-eb@opensciencegrid.org][osg-eb@opensciencegrid.org]] if you are interested in any of these programs. 

---++ Sign-up for education and training events
To learn more about education and training events, sign up for our events announcements MailingList. 
To sign up for grid related events notifications, send out a message to listserv@opensciencegrid.org with no subject and the following line in the message body:
subscribe grid-edu-announce@opensciencegrid.org First_Name Last_Name
