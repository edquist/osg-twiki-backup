<td colspan=2 valign="top" bgcolor=#21325D><font color=#FFFFFF><b>South Africa</b></font></td>
</tr>
<td colspan=2 valign="top">

We are working with a group of faculty and graduate students from the [[http://web.wits.ac.za/][Witwatersrand (Wits) University]] and University of Johannesburg, South Africa and [[http://www.columbia.edu/][Columbia University]] to establish an OSG cluster at Wits/UJ (University of Johannesburg) and to involve scientists in using it for their research. <br>
An update (as of Feb 2009) on the SA activity can be found [[%ATTACHURL%/SA-update.pdf][here]]. <br><br>
Training and education events organized by OSG in partnership with South African team:
   * [[http://physics.uj.ac.za/events/2009/Easter_Workshop][Easter Research Cluster Training Workshop]], April 9-14, 2009 in Johannesburg, followed by post-workshop assistance on porting application to the the newly established UJ cluster.
   * [[http://neo.phys.wits.ac.za/gridschool][South African Grid School]] July 23-25, 2008 in Johannesburg
<br>
</td>
</tr>
