---+ Fun Things to do in Madison

   * [[http://www.union.wisc.edu/][Memorial Union]] is a great place to hang out, just a few blocks from the !DoubleTree; many people like to get a drink and relax on the Terrace, which looks out over [[http://www.soils.wisc.edu/asig/webcam.html][Lake Monona]]
      * Check [[http://www.union.wisc.edu/events.aspx][the Wisconsin Union events calendar]]
   * [[http://www.state-st.com][State Street]] is a student-friendly street and outdoor pedestrian mall, a couple blocks from the !DoubleTree, with tons of great businesses — restaurants, stores, bars, and more
   * Catch a movie at [[http://www.orpheumtheatre.net/][the Orpheum Theatre]]
   * Check out [[http://www.overturecenter.com/][The Overture Center for the Arts]]
   * Read [[http://www.thedailypage.com/][The Isthumus Daily Page]] for the latest events
   * Go to a [[http://www.wcoconcerts.org/performances/concerts-on-the-square/][free outdoor classical music "Concert on the Square" on Wednesday]], by the State Capitol building
