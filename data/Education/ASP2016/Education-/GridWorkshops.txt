The [[http://www.utb.edu/][University of Texas at Brownsville and Texas Southmost College]] (UTB/TSC), the [[http://www.griphyn.org/][Grid Physics Network]] (!GriPhyN), the [[http://www.ivdgl.org/][International Virtual Data Grid Laboratory]] (iVDGL), and the [[http://www.grids-center.org/][Grid Research Integration Deployment and Support Center]] (GRIDS) sponsored a one-week intensive summer workshop program in grid computing and its application in scientific data analysis. This school was held on [[http://www.sopadre.com/index.php][South Padre Island]], a resort located on the Gulf of Mexico, twenty miles from downtown Brownsville, Texas during the week of July 11, 2005 - July 15, 2005
