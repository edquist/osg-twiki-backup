---+++ Overview
The material for this workshop is under construction. You may though find it useful to look at the material introduced at the [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/SummerGridWorkshop/SummerGridSyllabus2006][Summer Grid Workshop 2006]]

---+++ Daily Schedule
Both days of the workshop will run from 9:00 am to 6:00pm, with a one hour lunch break. Each day will consist of four lectures (of 30 minutes), each followed by longer hands-on laboratory time (90 minutes). Lecturers and assistants will provide assistance during lab sessions and be available for discussion and follow-up.

A Saturday night social event, to be announced, will be planned. 

---++Saturday March 24, 2007 9:00am - 6:00pm
