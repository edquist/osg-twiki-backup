---++ Lecture and Lab Schedule
<!-- PDFSTART -->
Both days of the workshop will run from 9:00 am to 6:00pm (8:00 AM Registration on Saturday), with a one hour lunch break. Each day will consist of four lectures (of  approximately 30-45 minutes), each followed by longer hands-on laboratory time (60-90 minutes). Lecturers and assistants will provide assistance during lab sessions and be available for discussion and follow-up.

A Saturday night social event will be held at Maggianos Italian restauarant, Grand and Clark.
