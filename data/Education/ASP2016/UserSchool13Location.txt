---+ School Location

The school will be held in the [[http://www.cs.wisc.edu/location.html][Computer Sciences Department]], Room 1240, located at [[http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&hl=en&geocode=&q=1210%20West%20Dayton.%2C%20Madison%2C%20Wisconsin][1210 West Dayton St., Madison, WI, 53706]]. It is a 15–20 minute walk from the DoubleTree Hotel.

---++ Getting From the DoubleTree to Computer Sciences

Walking:
   1. Exit the DoubleTree onto West Johnson Street
   2. Walk west on West Johnson Street, towards campus
   3. Turn Left at North Park Street, walk 1 block
   4. Turn Right at West Dayton Street, continue west on Dayton
   5. The main entrance to the Computer Sciences building is on the right, midway down the 1200 block

*Free* hotel shuttle:
   * In the event of inclement weather, the DoubleTree will provide free shuttle service between the hotel and Computer Sciences

*Free* buses:
   * The official [[http://www.cityofmadison.com/metro/schedules/][Madison Metro Bus page]] provides route maps and times for all Madison buses.  There is no convenient route from the DoubleTree to Computer Sciences, but the buses are a great way to get around town when walking or using the hotel shuttle are not an option.

---++ Room 1240, Computer Sciences Building

School sessions and breakfasts are held in *Room 1240*:

   1. Enter the Computer Sciences building through the glass doors on Dayton Street
   1. Turn left and go through two sets of double doors
   1. Walk down the hallway just a bit
   1. Room 1240 is on the right, up the ramp or a few stairs

The Computer Sciences building is a bit confusing — we will post signs to help you find your way. You can always ask people for directions if you get lost.
