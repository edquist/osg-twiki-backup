__Do you have previous experience as a sysadmin ?__

__If so, read below...__

[[http://www.opensciencegrid.org][The Open Science Grid]] (OSG),  [[www.clemson.edu][Clemson University]]  and the [[http://www.ci.uchicago.edu][Computation Institute]] of [[http://www.uchicago.edu][The University of Chicago]] present a two day training course in grid installation. 



School participants will work with grid computing experts during the 2-day training. The workshop will focus on enabling the use of the national     
cyberinfrastructure -[[http://www.opensciencegrid.org][The Open Science Grid]]

*Prerequisites*. Knowledge of Unix is preffered, but we encourage non-Unix users to attend.
 

Applicants should have at least intermediate programming skills (one to two     
semesters experience in C/C++, Java, Perl, and/or Python) and hands-on          
experience with UNIX / Linux in a networked environment.
