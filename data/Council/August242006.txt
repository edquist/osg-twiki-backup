---+++OSG Council August Meeting Goals:

   * Have a set of documents and business practices defined for the OSG Council 
   * How to take advantage of possible funding to fold into the OSG project plan 
   * How to be position OSG to leverage future funding opportunities 

---+++Meeting Logistics

Location
Meeting will be held in the Physics-Astronomy Building (PAB) on the University of Washington, Seattle campus. The PAB consists of three wings. "A" contains auditoria, "B" is the long rectangular one, and "C" is the taller tower.  Between the three wings is an open plaza with physics labs below.  More details are at the Collaboration web site, http://www.opensciencegrid.org/cms/?pid=1000103.

Teleconference
