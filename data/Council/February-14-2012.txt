---+++ Status Report from OSG Council Co-Chairs

     1)  Report from the OSG  Executive Director Selection Sub-committee

   * Sub-committee recommends the Council vote to approve the following: 



   
---------------------------------------------------------------
Item 1: 

Proposed variance to OSG Executive Director election procedure applicable to the 2012 Executive Director election


_After the close of open nominations to the position of Executive Director, the Executive Director Selection Sub-committee will advance to the OSG Council the list of qualified candidates among those nominated who are willing to stand for election.The voting period for the election will begin one month after the candidate announcement and will follow the "non-meeting election process" specified in the OSG Consortium Voting Procedures document (OSG-DocDB-311). Prior to the election, the Executive Director candidate(s) will make a presentation to the Council during the regular meeting of the Council in February._


_The transition period to the new ED will begin April 1, 2012 and extend though May 31, 2012. The term of the new Executive Director will begin on June 1, 2012.2012._

---------------------------------------------------------------


Item 2: 

Proposed extension to the term of the OSG Executive Director

_The term of the current OSG Executive Director, Ruth Pordes, is extended from April 1, 2012 through May 31, 2012._


-----------------------------------------------------------------

Item 3:  

Proposed extension to the term of OSG Council Chair


_The term of the current OSG Council Co-chairs, Paul Avery and Rick Snider, is extended through May 31, 2012. The election of the new Council Chair will take place during the March 2012 Face-to-face meeting of the Council._
