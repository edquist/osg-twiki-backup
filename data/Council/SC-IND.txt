---++ Subcommittee to discussion OSG Strategy with respect to Relationships with Industry

---+++ Draft Charter 


 
A sub-committee of the Open Science Grid Consortium Council is asked to write a proposal for the Consortium’s strategic goals for industry relationships and engagement over the next 5-10 years:
   * Clarify the goals, principles and approaches for the OSG Consortiums interactions with Commercial organizations, communities and individuals.
   * Make a proposal for the nature of Commercial organizations with which OSG should engage.
   * Make a proposal for Commercial Organization Activities and Interactions Useful for the OSG in 2020.
 
The scope should include:
   * Criteria for discussion with Commercial entities
   * Articulation of benefits of working with Commercial entiites
   * Principles of, user potential to benefit from, and cost analyses for OSG purchasing access to commercial resources on behalf of existing or future stakeholders.
   * Recommendation on a set of stakeholder interests, set of commercial providers and proposed activities that the OSG could approach
   * Recommendation on whether OSG should change the current policy of not supporting commercial users or applications on OSG resources.
   * Recommendation on pursuit of commercial providers to support other OSG production services (at the moment we will rely on DigiCert for certificate provisioning).
   * Specific attention to potential for OSG engagement in SBIR and STTR programs.
