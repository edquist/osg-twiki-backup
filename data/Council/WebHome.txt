The OSG Council is the governing body of the Consortium responsible for the program of work and ensuring effective benefit to the scientific mission of the stakeholders. The Consortium includes all contributors to OSG; the Council is made of those Consortium members making significant contributions and commitments to the success of OSG. It makes all strategic decisions, which are typically discussed first in and recommended by the Executive Board.

Send email to contact the [[mailto:osg-council@opensciencegrid.org][OSG-Council]]

   * [[August242006][Council meeting 24th August 2006 in Seattle]]
   * Telephone meetings of the Council are organized more or less weekly.
   * The next face to face meeting of the Council is March 8th 2007 at the OSG Consortium Allhands meeting at UCSD.
