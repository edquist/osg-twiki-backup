*On February 9th, 2010 Kent Blackburn this Statement of Intent to run with Paul Avery as OSG Council Co-Chairs for a second term:*

<verbatim>
Dear OSG Council,

As many of you heard in today's OSG Council Teleconference, Paul Avery
and I have officially conveyed our intent for candidacy in the election
for the next term as OSG Council Co-Chairs.

This election will be held at the March Council Face-to-Face meeting at
Fermilab.

According to recently approved recommendations for Action Item 8 adopted
a on January 28th, 2010, the deadline for announcing intent for
candidacy is end of the day today. At this time there have been no other
announcements of intent.

Sincerely,
Kent Blackburn

</verbatim>

-- Main.KentBlackburn - 24 Feb 2010
