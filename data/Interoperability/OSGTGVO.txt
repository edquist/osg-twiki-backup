---+ OSGTG VO Information

%TOC%

The OSGTG VO is a group of users from various OSG VOs participating in the OSG-TeraGrid Interoperability effort.  

---++ VOMS/VOMRS server

VOMS for OSGTG is hosted at Texas Tech University.

The VOMRS server is accessible via the URL https://scnh005.phys.ttu.edu:8443/vo/OSGTG/vomrs .
