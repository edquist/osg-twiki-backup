---+ OSGTG VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo Information

The OSGTG VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo is a group of users from various OSG VOs participating in the OSG-TeraGrid Interoperability effort.  

---++ VirtualOrganizations/VOInfoMS/VirtualOrganizations/VOInfoMRS server

VirtualOrganizations/VOInfoMS for OSGTG is hosted at Texas Tech University.

The VirtualOrganizations/VOInfoMRS server is accessible via the URL https://scnh005.phys.ttu.edu:8443/vo/OSGTG/vomrs .
