---+Migrating from facct to the Gratia psacct probe

<OL>
<LI>Start the Gratia ps accounting service:

     =/etc/rc.d/init.d/gratia-psacct=

<LI>Remove or comment-out the fermilab accounting entries from *root's crontab* (or
elsewhwere if appropriate):

<verbatim>#00 23 * * * /usr/lib/acct/runacct 1>/var/adm/acct/nite/acct.log 2>&1
#00 05 1 * * /usr/lib/acct/monacct 1>/var/adm/acct/fiscal/monacct.log 2>&1</verbatim>

<LI> Run:

     =chkconfig facct off=

<LI>Make sure =ProbeConfig= is customized according to README.

<LI>Run facct-catchup from the =<gratia-top-dir>/probe/psacct directory=.

</OL>
