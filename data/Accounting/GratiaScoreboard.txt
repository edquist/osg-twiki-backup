---# Introduction 
This page collects information about the Scoreboard project, to make accounting information available to Intensity Frontier experiments at Fermilab.

---# Status 
The scope of the project is currently not set.
There are some static pages with examples of which information can be made available (Scoreboard)
The project is in planning phase and here collected are some documents:
   * [[http://cd-docdb.fnal.gov/cgi-bin/ShowDocument?docid=5303][Evaluation of !XDMoD]]
   * [[http://cd-docdb.fnal.gov/cgi-bin/ShowDocument?docid=5304][IF Accounting Survey results]]
