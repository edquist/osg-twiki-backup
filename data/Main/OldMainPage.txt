---+!! OSG Collaborative Work Area Home Page


This is the home page of OSG's TWiki collaborative work area. Work in OSG is done in "Activities" that are coordinated through the TWiki, and also through regularly scheduled phone meetings and mailing lists. New members of OSG are encouraged to participate in any or all of these.


Our collaborative work area comprises separate webs for each of the activities, for documentation, and for other "hands-on" topics.
