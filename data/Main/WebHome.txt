---++ Introduction
Our collaborative workspace is this TWiki, of which you are at the home page.  Work in OSG is done in "Activities" coordinated through this TWiki, regularly scheduled phone conferences, and [[http://www.opensciencegrid.org/?pid=1000237][mailing lists]].  All of these communication modes are open to new members. 

Our TWiki comprises separate "webs" for each of the technical activities, for documentation, and for other "hands-on" topics.  Technical activities typically keep meeting minutes, to-do lists, and other administrative info in their webs.  

For help on using TWiki, see [[GetStaredWithTwiki][Getting started using TWiki]].

Please send suggestions, corrections, and other comments about the OSG website or TWiki to osg-docs at opensciencegrid dot org.
