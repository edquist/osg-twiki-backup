---+!! OSG Collaborative Work Area Home Page

This is the home page of OSG's TWiki collaborative work area. Work in OSG is done in "Activities", coordinated through this TWiki, regularly scheduled phone conferences, and [[http://www.opensciencegrid.org/?pid=1000237][mailing lists]]. All of these communication modes are open to new members.

Our collaborative work area comprises separate "webs" for each of the activities, for documentation, and for other "hands-on" topics.
