---++ Introduction
This is the home page of "OSG at Work", our TWiki collaborative workspace.  Work in OSG is done in "Activities", coordinated through this TWiki, regularly scheduled phone conferences, and [[http://www.opensciencegrid.org/?pid=1000237][mailing lists]].  All of these communication modes are open to new members. 

Our TWiki comprises separate "webs" for each of the activities, for documentation, and for other "hands-on" topics.
