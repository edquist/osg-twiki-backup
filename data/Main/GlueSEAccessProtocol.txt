---++GlueSEAccessProtocol Attributes
The !AccessProtocol describes allowed ways to transfer &#64257;les to and from an SE.

dn: !GlueSEAccessProtocolLocalID=gsiftp,GlueSEUniqueID=SE_FQDN,mds-vo-name=local,o=grid 

*NOTES*:
   1. Currently !AccessProtocol  is configures to be gsiftp for both srm and disk based SE
