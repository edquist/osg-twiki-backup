---++Introduction

The Open Science Grid stack is based on the Virtual Data Toolkit. For OSG 0.1.5, we are using VDT %OSG_ITB_VDT_VERSION%.

---++Documentation

The main VDT web site is at: http://www.cs.wisc.edu/vdt

Links to all of the VDT documentation is at: http://www.cs.wisc.edu/vdt/documentation.html

Documentation for this release of the VDT is at: http://www.cs.wisc.edu/vdt/releases/%OSG_ITB_VDT_VERSION%/index.html

You can contact the VDT team by emailing vdt-support@cs.wisc.edu.
