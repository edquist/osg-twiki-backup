---++Schedule

This twiki organizes the follow-up deployment of ITB 0.1.5 during the week of April 18.  This will provide the final run-up for the provisioning activity.

   * Release Candidate workshop, April 14-15: RelCanItb015
   * Telecon meetings Tuesday, April 19 to Friday, April 22 
      * Coordinates: Noon Central, 1-510-665-5437 #1212
   * Expect final release of VDT 1.3.5 Monday morning.
   * Monday afternoon, pending the VDT release, announce "release" ITB 0.1.5: ItbRel015.
   * Communication of any and all problems via osg-int@opensciencegrid.org
   * Monday afternoon: start installing on remaining ITB sites
   * Validations from services and applications to continue all week.
   * Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday there will be noon Central telecons to handshake and discuss problems.
      * [[MinutesApril20][Wednesday 4/20/05 Minutes]]
      * [[MinutesApril21][Thursday 4/21/05 Minutes]]
   * *THURSDAY, 4/21/05* we'll use the normal timeslot: 2:30pm Central
   * Accumulate results of all tests, readied for OSG deployment discussion Friday afternoon.  Continued improvements to documentation.
   * Concurrent, the Provisioning Activity will start ramping up in preparation for deployment.


_*Note to ITB site admins*_:
   * We are asking ALL ITB site admins to register with twiki (see home page) and FIX and clarify documentation during this period.  This is part of the expected effort required for participation in the ITB activity.  Thanks in advance!
