---++ Updates and Issues from Miron


   * GridEx applications is running on a few ITB sites. Jeff and Alan would like to expand to more ITB sites
     to track and diagnose problems. Especially needed are non-Condor batch queueing systems, PBS, LSF and SGE.
     Information and history of the GridEx application are on the GridEx page. 
   * ITB sites need more formal procedures for announcing problems, service blog?
     ITB sites should be culled on some regular schedule.
   * Errors, esp Globus Error 129, need detailed review from site admins to understand the
     core issue and if this error creates low throughtput for other applications.
