In the following, the prefix may differ according to how your iptables file currently reads.

For postgres, add these lines to /etc/sysconfig/iptables
<pre>
-A RH-Firewall-1-INPUT  -m state --state NEW -p tcp -m tcp --dport 5432 -j ACCEPT
-A RH-Firewall-1-OUTPUT  -m state --state ESTABLISHED -p tcp -m tcp --sport 5432 -j ACCEPT
</pre>
