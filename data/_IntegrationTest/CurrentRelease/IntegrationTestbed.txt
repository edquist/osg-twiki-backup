---+++Installation and Configuration
During initial period of installation and configuration, send mail to the OSG integration mailing list, osg-int at opensciencegrid dot org.  
	* An archive of communication is <a href="http://listserv.fnal.gov/archives/osg-int.html">given here</a>.

---+++Operations Center
	* After the initial installation phase, the iVDGL Grid Operations Center will provide coordination support for the OSG ITB
		* The iGOC phone number is +1 317 278 9699 (rarely used)
		* The iGOC email address is igoc at ivdgl.org.  Sending mail to this address will open a trouble ticket.  Be sure to state that the problem is in reference to OSG-ITB (OSG Integration Test Bed) 
		* In addition, a <a href="http://igoc.ivdgl.indiana.edu/test/index.php"> web form </a> may be used to issue a trouble ticket.
		* All trouble tickets will be reviewed at the weekly iVDGL operations meeting, see http://igoc.ivdgl.indiana.edu/.
