---++Introduction

---++ Schedule

   * April 17: Target date for deployment of the OSG 0.4.1 production release  %RED% (delayed till ~ May 1) %ENDCOLOR%
   * April 7:  ITB release candidate given to provisioning team %RED% (delayed till ~ April 21th) %ENDCOLOR%
   * April 3: Identify fixes and plan for [[ItbRel037][ITB 0.3.7]] (%BLUE% in progress %ENDCOLOR%)
   * March 20: Start of validations run for of deployed services and Trash/Trash/Trash/Trash/VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo applications %GREEN% started March 23 %ENDCOLOR%
   * March 15: Date of ITB virtual integration meeting for OSG 0.3.6 %GREEN% done %ENDCOLOR%
   * March 8: All documentation on services and applications for installation, configuration and validation on ITB delivered. %GREEN% Done %ENDCOLOR%
   * Feb 23: Freeze on ITB 0.3.6 *pre-release*. ITB packages and initial documentation ready. %GREEN% (delayed till ~ March 9) %ENDCOLOR%  (%RED% Release of ITB 0.3.6 on Feb 16 %ENDCOLOR%)
   * Feb 16: ITB 0.3.6 pre-release available %GREEN% (done) %ENDCOLOR%
   * Feb 16: VDT 1.3.10 pre-release available %GREEN% (done) %ENDCOLOR%
   * Jan 31: ITB 0.3.6 Cache but not released until VDT 1.3.10 available %GREEN% (done) %ENDCOLOR%
