---++Introduction

This a short description of the OSG Mailing Lists for operations. 
---++Mailing List Overview


   * GOC@opensciencegrid.org  --> Support contact for OSG Operations Core Support
   * SUPPORT@opensciencegrid.org -> GOC
   * TROUBLE@opensciencegrid.org -> GOC
   * HELP@opensciencegrid.org -> GOC

 This is the support contact point for OSG Operations Core Support; it's used by the Support Centers to report problems and interact with the core Grid Operations Center. GOC@ is not the contact point for users and system administrators to contact their Support Center. Support requests that are misdirected to this address will be forwarded to the appropriate Support Center along with a notification to the person making the request. The GOC@ roster includes contacts points for each participant in OSG core support.

   * OSG-GENERAL@opensciencegrid.org 

This is the Community support list for OSG 

   * OPERATIONS --> OSG-OPERATIONS
   * OSG-OPERATIONS@opensciencegrid.org
 

 Operations Activities is an open mailing list for discussion of OSG Operations matters among users, system administrators, service providers, and operations support. It is not expected that every grid user is subscribed to this list.

   * OSG-SITES@opensciencegrid.org

 This list is used for sending notices to all resource providers.  Administrators should subscribe to this list when installing the OSG software or registering a resource.

   * VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo-ADMINS@opensciencegrid.org

 This list is used for sending notices to all VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo managers and VirtualOrganizations/VOInfoMS administrators. Adminstrators should subscribe to this list when registering a VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo and VirtualOrganizations/VOInfoMS service.
 
   * OPS-STATUS@opensciencegrid.org

This list is used to send out notices of status reports and service changes to the OSG infrastructure.

---++Joining a Mailing List

To SUBSCRIBE to a mailing list called MYLIST

   1. Send an e-mail message to
   2. listserv@opensciencegrid.org Leave the subject line blank
   3. Type "SUBSCRIBE MYLIST FIRSTNAME LASTNAME" (without the quotation marks) in the body of your message. 

      You may also use "JOIN MYLIST FIRSTNAME LASTNAME" (without the quotation marks) in the body of your message.

Note: Some mailing lists are set up so that people are not allowed to subscribe themselves. If you attempt to subscribe yourself to such a list, you will get a message telling you that your request has been forwarded to the list owner.
