---++Introduction
This section provides some hints on troubleshooting problems identified in the site verification process using the site_verify.pl script.

---++Authentication (running a job on the gatekeeper fork queue)
The authentication verification is checking  to see if you can run a job on your gatekeeper by executing on the fork queue.  You need a grid proxy as a prerequisite and therefore your certificate credentials..
<verbatim>
	  grid-proxy-init
	  globus-job-run {your-gate-keeper]/jobmanager-fork  /bin/hostname
</verbatim>

Upon success, the exit status will be zero and the host name of the gatekeeper node will be displayed..
