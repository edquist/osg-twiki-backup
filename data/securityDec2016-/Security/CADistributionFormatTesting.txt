---++Testing of new CA distribution format on ITB

      * gatekeeper
      * gsiftp
      * RSV probes
      * vdt-update-certs
      * vdt-ca-manage
      * fetch-crl

   3. GUMS
   4. VOMS
   5. OSG client
      * globus client tools
      * srm client tools
      * Condor-G
   6. WN Client
   7. pilot jobs framework
