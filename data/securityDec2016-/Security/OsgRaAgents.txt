---++ Email addresses.
---+++ Public email addresses.
   * osg-ra@opensciencegrid.org - The public contact point for the OSG RA with the rest of the DOEGRids PKI as well as the OSG community. 

---+++ Internal (private) email addresses.
   * osg-ra-agents@opensciencegrid.org - list with all of the agents for OSG. 
   * osg-ra-gridadmin@opensciencegrid.org - list of all people authorized for OSG with gridadmin privilege. 
   * osg-ra-log@opensciencegrid.org - archive mail list to record actions of the RA and agents. Agents and gridadmin should have privilege to post to this list but not many people need to be subscribed to it.
