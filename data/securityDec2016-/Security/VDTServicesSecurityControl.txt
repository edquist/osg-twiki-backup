---+ List of VDT Services for security control

   1. VDT web site (vdt.cs.wisc.edu)
      * Pacman caches (.pacman files + software)
      * Native package repositories
      * VDT CA certificate distribution
      * Subversion repository with https access
      * Configuration for our VDT Test Cluster
      * Documentation
      * Relies on UW CS systems staff for underlying services (apache, AFS, etc.) 
   1.  VDT ticket system (crt.cs.wisc.edu)
      * VDT support
      * Internal VDT "to do tickets"
      * VDT security tickets
      * Relies on UW CS systems staff for underlying services (apache, Postgres, etc.)
   1. Build and test facility (not owned by us, but where we build most software)
   1. Two OSG sites:
      1. VDT-ITB: our ITB site, where we do VDT testing
      1. OSG-EDU: Our production OSG site, primarily intended for educational use, but occasionally other uses. (It has a rather small cluster behind it.)
   1. UW VDT Test Cluster: on private network, must access via bastion computer. Used for automated daily testing.
      1. A postgres database server for collecting results from our VDT test cluster. It's on the public Internet, maintained by our UW CS systems staff
   1. Fermilab storage test cluster (tests Bestman, Hadoop, dCache, etc.) 
   1. Pivotal Tracker (Externally provided tool for tracking internal work, [[https://www.pivotaltracker.com/projects/116309]])
