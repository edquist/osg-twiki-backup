---+ OSG Security Incident Response Procedure Site Checklist

---++ Discovery
| Local security team |\
if applicable, inform within 4 hours |
| OSG security team |\
Inform within 4 hours, either: <br>\
   * [[https://ticket.grid.iu.edu/goc/security][submit a ticket]] <br>\
   * Send email to <security@opensciencegrid.org> <br>\
   * Call the Grid Operations Center (GOC) at +1 317-278-9699 |

---++ Containment
| Affected hosts | If applicable and possible, isolate from the network within 1 working day |

---++ Confirmation
| Incident | Confirm with local security team and/or OSG security team that this is actually a security incident. |

---++ Downtime Announcement
| Service Downtime | If applicable, announce to affected people that the involved systems are down for "Security operations" within 1 working day |

---++ Analysis
| Evidence | Collect as appropriate |
| Incident Analysis | Perform as appropriate |
| Requests from OSG Security Team | Follow up within 4 hours |

---++ Debriefing
| Post-mortem Incident Report | Prepare and distribute to security@opensciencegrid.org within 1 month |

---++ Restore Normal Operation
| After incident handling is complete | Restore per site standards |
| Procedures and Documentation | Update as appropriate after analysis results. |
