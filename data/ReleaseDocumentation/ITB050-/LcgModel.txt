---+ The LCG Model for Local Storage
%STARTINCLUDE%
%EDITTHIS%
The LCG Model follows a standard for storage from the [[http://lcg.web.cern.ch/LCG/][LHC Computing Grid]] 

The LCG Model includes the following storage areas:
   * OSG_GRID
   * OSG_APP
   * OSG_DEFAULT_SE
   * OSG_WN_TMP 

A dataflow example may be: 
   * site admin intervention or external transfer 
   * srmcp: OSG_DEFAULT_SE->OSG_WN_TMP
   * job execution
   * srmcp: OSG_WN_TMP->OSG_DEFAULT_SE
   * external stage-out

   $ %X% *%RED%WARNING%ENDCOLOR%*: Current applications depending on normal file access to a shared space may require non-trivial changes. There may also be issues with jobmanagers that use a shared directory to transfer the executable or data.
%STOPINCLUDE%
