---++ Contacts

   * For emergencies the OSG Grid Operation Center provides extended support (6am-9pm EST). GOC can be reached via:
      * Phone +1 317-278-9699 
      * Email goc@opensciencegrid.org 
      * [[https://ticket.grid.iu.edu/goc/open][Web interface of the ticket system]]
   * Trouble tickets can be opened with the Grid Operations Center by mailing to goc@opensciencegrid.org or visiting this [[https://ticket.grid.iu.edu/goc/open][GOCTicket link]].
   * Send email to osg-sites@opensciencegrid.org for basic installation and configuration questions.
   * For storage services support, email osg-storage@opensciencegrid.org.
   * osg-int@opensciencegrid.org is a community list used by OSG developers and integrators.

---++ OSG Sites Coordination Group

The [[SiteCoordination.WebHome][Sites Coordination]] group meets (via telecon) on a monthly basis, Thursdays, usually on the second Thursday of the month, at 11am Central.  Announcements will be made to osg-sites@opensciencegrid.org.  There are additionally face to face meetings of site administrators, roughly quarterly.

The Site Coordination group provides regular office hours support available weekly on a regular schedule using a Campfire Web chat. 
   * [[/SiteCoordination/ChatCalendar][Here]] you can find the current schedule
   * [[http://integrationcloud.campfirenow.com/6e62e][Click here]] to connect to the chat as a guest
   * You can request also login access to be able to see transcripts of previous sessions
