Key Initiatives
   1. perfSONAR 3.4.1 released yesterday. Autoupdate is on by default: learning from Shellshock vulnerability, which cause perfSONAR downtimes.
   2. Progress on OSG production infrastructure at GOC: MaDDash (metrics), OMD (service monitoring), and Datastore deployed. As sites deploy perfSONAR 3.4.1, production data will be populated.
