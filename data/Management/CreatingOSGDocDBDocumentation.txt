OSG administrative staff will assist with keeping up to date OSG Doc DB and creating documents as requested.

---++Scope
The OSG administrator staff will assist with keeping all OSG documentation associated with the correct topics and adding new topics as requested. For futher information on OSG policies please reference: https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Management/OSGPoliciesProcedure.

---++How to create a new document in OSG Doc DB
   * Open OSG Doc DB at:  https://osg-docdb.opensciencegrid.org:440/cgi-bin/DocumentDatabase/

   * Click create document (create a new from either PC or Web)

   * Under “Document Addition”  please complete all fields required as marked by an asterisk.

   * For topic location please view a detailed list of current topics and definitions at: http://osg-docdb.opensciencegrid.org/topics_cert.html

   * After you have selected your topic please submit the document at which time it will be uploaded in the OSG Doc DB and will be assigned a document number for your reference.

   * If a topic does not exist please send mailto:docdb-adm@fnal.gov .


---++Updating or Adding a Document on Doc DB
Please reference: https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Management/UpdatingorAddingaDocumentonDocDB.
