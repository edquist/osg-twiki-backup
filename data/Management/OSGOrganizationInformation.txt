---+!! OSG Organization

The OSG Consortium builds and operates the OSG project. Consortium members contribute effort and resources to the common infrastructure, with the goal of giving scientists from many fields access to shared resources worldwide.  

The OSG Consortium [[http://osg-docdb.opensciencegrid.org/cgi-bin/RetrieveFile?docid=25&extension=pdf][Charter]] and [[http://osg-docdb.opensciencegrid.org/cgi-bin/RetrieveFile?docid=70&extension=pdf][By-Laws]] explain how the OSG Consortium works. 

The Council governs the consortium ensuring that the OSG benefits the scientific mission of its stakeholders. 

The Executive Team manages the OSG project, which is organized into technical team areas.
