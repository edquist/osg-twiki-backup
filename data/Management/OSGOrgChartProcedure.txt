Maintaining OSG Organizational Chart

---++Scope
The OSG Administrator will maintain and keep up to date the OSG organizational charts on the web as neded.
 
---++Procedure

   * Ruth to send powerpoint of current chart to be made into a jpg. to be uploaded to the web
   * Jemise will follow up with Ruth monthly for any changes to the chart made
