The OSG Indico Calendar is the most widely used centralized location for information on OSG meetings.

---++Scope
The OSG administrator staff will assist with creating, updating and maintaining the OSG Indico calendar.

---++Creating meetings in Indico
The administrative staff will assist in creating meetings in Indico as requested or notified.  To view Indico please visit: http://indico.fnal.gov/.  For first time users you will need to create an Indico account at: https://indico.fnal.gov/signIn.py?returnURL=http%3A%2F%2Findico.fnal.gov%2Findex.py%3FcategId%3D0. 


---++Updating meetings in Indico
The administrative staff will also assist in updating meetings as needed and or requested.
For further information with instructions on how to use Indico please view: http://indico.fnal.gov/help.py.
