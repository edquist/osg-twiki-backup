---++Scope
Please ensure to follow the steps below for any participant support payments.

---++Step by Step Process:
   *Prior to the event please send an email to Cathy Richard at cathy@cs.wisc.edu with the following information:*
   * Name of the event and website address
   * Names of the participants and their email addresses
   * Nationalities so we are able to obtain the proper paperwork
   * Any limits on the amount of the reimbursement and any other limits of the reimbursement
   * Account Number
   * A copy of the budget sheet

  *Permanent residents and US citizens are treated as the same*

B1/BT or WB visa requirements:  passport, I-94, visa, social security number, ITIN (individual taxpayer identification number) or copy of filed ITIN application; W8-BEN form
 
 F1:  All of the above plus a letter from their institution's international office with the following information, Name and dates of conference attending, the I-20 form
 
 J1:  Please contact me right away, as there are very specific instructions this visa holder.
 
 H1B:  Same as B1 WB, plus I-797

At the time that Cathy is notified she will forward a copy of the travel worksheet that will need to be completed by each attendee.

If these basic guidelines are followed then all future reimbursements whould go through faster and smoother!

_Please note that there may be individual adjustments depending on the specific event_

For any questions or concerns in the process plese send mail to Cathy Richard.
