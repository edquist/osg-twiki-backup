---+ OSG Software Release Series Support Policy

As of November 2013, the OSG software is available in multiple, parallel release series. For example, in early 2014 there are OSG 3.1 and 3.2 series of RPM releases available from separate repositories.

This document describes the OSG policy for supporting release series, especially older ones. <span style="color: #F60;">As of 20 February 2014, this document is still in draft status.</span>

   * First 6 months, backport by request or at discretion of Software
   * First 3 months, preference is to backport all changes
   * After 6 months, backport bug fixes at request of Production or critical security fixes
   * At 6 months, Production will propose an EOL date no less than 6 months later
