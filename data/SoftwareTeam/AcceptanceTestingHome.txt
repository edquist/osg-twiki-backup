---+ Acceptance Testing Procedures for OSG Components.

The OSG Release Team collects and maintains testing procedures for major components in the OSG Sofware Stack. These components are enumerated in the [[ComponentHome][Component notes]]. We have gotten a start with some [[SoftwareTeam.TestingRecipes][Manual testing recipes]]. These test should verify that basic functionality of the component works in typically deployed configurations.
