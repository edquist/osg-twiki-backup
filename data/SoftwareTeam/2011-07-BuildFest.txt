---++ Goals


<table>
<tr>
<td width=50%>
---+++ Primary Goals
   1. Get the OSG Software Team synchronized and working towards our strategy for packaging the OSG Software Stack as RPMs. 
   1. Train members of the team so they are all capable of making RPMs with the VDT's infrastructure
</td>
<td width=50%>
---+++ Secondary Goals
   a. We also want to package as much of the VDT as we can. How much is realistic to do during a three day meeting? We'll see. 
   a. If time allows, we will discuss testing process for RPM-based releases.
   a. If time allows, we will discuss documentation
</td>
</tr>
</table>

---++ Expectations
Our move to RPMs is moving very quickly. As such:
   * The agenda for this meeting is rough and will evolve
   * Our technical understanding is not yet complete. We do not have all answers. Some we will decide during this meeting.
