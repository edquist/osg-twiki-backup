---++ SRM v2.2 client status (LBNL implementation)

!SRM-client toolkit is a full implementation of SRM v2.2, developed by Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, as generic SRM v2.2 clients. They have been tested for all current SRM v2.2 implementations such as !BeStMan, CASTOR, dCache, DPM, SRM/SRB and !StoRM. They are continuously being tested for compatibility and interoperability.  Note: These do not work with the previous SRM v1.1 servers.

   * <a name="sample_client"><b>Sample client runs</b></a>
      * Note: more samples are coming...
      * Assuming that SRM_Service_Endpoint=srm://srm.lbl.gov:8443/srm/v2/manager
      * Assuming that SRM_PATH=/pnfs/data/osgdemo
         * ping
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-ping $SRM_Service_Endpoint
         * put
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-copy file:////tmp/test.data $SRM_Service_Endpoint\?SFN=$SRM_PATH/osg.test.data
         * ls
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-ls $SRM_Service_Endpoint\?SFN=$SRM_PATH/osg.test.data
         * get
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-copy $SRM_Service_Endpoint\?SFN=$SRM_PATH/osg.test.data file:////tmp/osg.data
         * rm
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-rm $SRM_Service_Endpoint\?SFN=$SRM_PATH/osg.test.data
         * mkdir
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-mkdir $SRM_Service_Endpoint\?SFN=$SRM_PATH/osg.test.dir
         * rmdir
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-rmdir $SRM_Service_Endpoint\?SFN=$SRM_PATH/osg.test.dir
         * space reservation
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-sp-reserve -serviceurl $SRM_Service_Endpoint -size 1000 -gsize 900 -lifetime 900
            * Or, $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-space -reserve -serviceurl $SRM_Service_Endpoint -size 1000 -gsize 900 -lifetime 900
         * space update
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-sp-update -serviceurl $SRM_Service_Endpoint -size 5000 -gsize 4000 -lifetime 300 -spacetoken V.10
            * Or, $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-space -serviceurl $SRM_Service_Endpoint -size 5000 -gsize 4000 -lifetime 300 -update V.10 
         * space tokens retrieval
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-sp-tokens -serviceurl $SRM_Service_Endpoint
            * Or, $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-space -serviceurl $SRM_Service_Endpoint -getspacetoken
         * space information retrieval
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-sp-info -serviceurl $SRM_Service_Endpoint -spacetoken V.10
            * Or, $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-space -serviceurl $SRM_Service_Endpoint -getspacetokenmeta V.10
         * put operation with the reserved space
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-copy file:////tmp/test.data $SRM_Service_Endpoint\?SFN=$SRM_PATH/osg.test2.data -spacetoken V.10
         * purge an SURL in the reserved space
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-sp-purge -serviceurl $SRM_Service_Endpoint -s "$SRM_Service_Endpoint?SFN=$SRM_PATH/osg.test2.data" -spacetoken V.10 
            * Or, $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-space -serviceurl $SRM_Service_Endpoint -s "$SRM_Service_Endpoint?SFN=$SRM_PATH/osg.test2.data" -purge V.10 
         * space release
            * $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-sp-release -serviceurl $SRM_Service_Endpoint -spacetoken V.10
            * Or, $SRM_HOME/bin/srm-space -serviceurl $SRM_Service_Endpoint -release V.10
