 !BeStMan release  (Alex)

---+++ Discussion
<!-- describe the discussion that took place -->
!BeStMan 2.21.2.i4 is released. It is already incorporated into ITB cache. 

Major changes:

Server:</br>
   1. Added a plugin for list of transfer servers 
   1. GUMS libraries are updated
   1. Retrieving FQAN from non-delegated credential is added
   1. --enable-backup and --with-backup-tag added  to preserve bestman configurattions
   1. Modified checksum value in hex

Srm clients 2:<br/>
   1. Added -noabortonfail option in srm-copy to disable abort when request failed
   1. Implemented exponential status polling in all status calls
   1. Made use of desiredTotalRequestTime and 
   server-returned remainingTotalRequestTime and estimatedWaitTIme
   1. Added better summary outputs for use case of srmCopy(srm, srm)

Alex has promised to start putting Release Notes on the web (outside of tar file).
