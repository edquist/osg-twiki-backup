---+++++ Relocating dCache

For testing purposes, it may be desirable to change the location of dCache from /opt/d-cache. In that case, the following steps must be taken. 

   * Modify the definition of ourHomeDir in bin/dcache-core, bin/dcache-pool, and bin/dcache-srm
   * Modifiy the definition of DCACHE_HOME in etc/srm_setup.env
   * Modify the definition of DCACHE_BASE_DIR in etc/node_config
   * Change ourHome in install/install.sh
   * Change the location of kpwdPath in etc/dcachesrm-gplazma.policy
   * Change the location of logArea in config/dCacheSetup
   * Run the install.sh script again.
