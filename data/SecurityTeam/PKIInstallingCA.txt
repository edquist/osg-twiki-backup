This page contains directions for installing the OSG PKI Certificate Authority (CA) certificate to establish trust in the OSG PKI. The OSG PKI Certificate Authority is the DigiCert Grid Certificate Authority and hence that is the CA certificate you need to install. There are several options for installing this CA certificate depending on your system and needs.

%TOC%

---+ Obtaining the CA Certificates

---++ Obtaining from OSG

The necessary !DigiCert CA certificate are available as part of the [[https://www.opensciencegrid.org/bin/view/Documentation/CaDistribution][OSG CA Distribution]].

---++ Obtaining from IGTF

The necessary !DigiCert CA certificates are available from the International Grid Trust Federation in its [[https://dist.eugridpma.org/distribution/igtf/current/][distribution]].

---++ Downloading Directly from !DigiCert

The CA certificates are available at the [[http://www.digicert-grid.com/][DigiCert-Grid Repository]].  The section titled "!DigiCert-Grid Grid-Only Trust CAs" contains the information and links for the Certificate Authority that issues the OSG PKI certiticates.

Download the !DigiCert Grid Root CA Certiticate in [[http://www.digicert-grid.com/DigiCertGridRootCA.cer][PEM]] and [[http://www.digicert-grid.com/DigiCertGridRootCA.der][DER (binary)]] formats.

Download the !DigiCert Grid CA-1 Certificate (this is a subordinate CA to the !DigiCert Grid Root CA) in [[http://www.digicert-grid.com/DigiCertGridCA-1.cer][PEM]] and [[http://www.digicert-grid.com/DigiCertGridRootCA.der][DER (binary)]] formats.

Note the downloaded PEM format files will appear with .cer file extensions (instead of .pem).


---+ Installing into a Web Browser

To import the CA certificates into your browser, download both the [[http://www.digicert-grid.com/DigiCertGridRootCA.cer][Root]] and [[http://www.digicert-grid.com/DigiCertGridCA-1.cer][CA-1]] PEM format files and then go into the Certificate Manager of your browser and use the Import function.  Make sure your import these certificates as Authorities.

---+ CRLs

You may also need certificate revocation lists (CRLS) for the !DigiCert CAs. These are  available at the following URLs. Note that you will need to set up some mechansism to regularly download and install these, as they regularly expire.

   * [[http://crl3.digicert.com/DigiCertGridRootCA.crl][DigiCert Grid Root CA CRL in DER]] format.
   * [[http://crl3.digicert.com/DigiCertGridCA-1.crl][DigiCert Grid CA-1 CRL in DER]] format.
