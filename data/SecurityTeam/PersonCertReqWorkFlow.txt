---+!! Workflow for Personal Certificate Requests

(in progress)

%TOC%


d15 5
---++ Workflow steps

   1. _Subscriber_ requests a personal certificate, using web browser or a script interface to the Registration Manager (RM)
   2. The RM sends email acknowledgement of the request to the subscriber and a notice of the request to all _Agents_. The email notice to _Agents_ contains the name of the VO (or institution) selected by the _subscriber_.
   3. An _Agent_ for the specified VO views the request web page and takes assignment of this request if they can process it.  Using the information provided for name, email address and phone numbers for _subscriber_ and _sponsor_ the _Agent_ determines one or more valid _Sponsors_ who may be able to confirm the request.  
   4. The _Agent_ contacts the _sponsor_ by telephone or email to ask for confirmation of the certificate request.
      a. Email to _Sponsor_, or telephone to _Sponsor_.
      a. _Sponsor_ makes direct contact with _subscriber_ to ask about certificate request.
   2. The RM sends email acknowledgement of the request to the subscriber and a notice of the request to _Agents_.
