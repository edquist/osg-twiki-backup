---++ Atlas (Armen & Xin)

   * General production status
      * After the machine development period LHC came back with very stable operation during the last week and collected luminosity for the week was 1.14fb-1. Current total collected luminosity for ATLAS is ~16.8 fb-1.
      * US ATLAS production during the last week was generally stable, at the average level around 20-22K running jobs. Reprocessing of the Express Stream was carried out over the week, with preparation for bulk reprocessing. There was some drop in the production level over the weekend, when reprocessing finished, and there was a delay with new task submissions. Monday the production was back to normal.
   * Job statistics for last week.      
      * Gratia report: 1.3M pilot jobs run on USATLAS sites, with CPU/walltime ratio of 88%
      * Real Jobs processed by US sites for last week, reported from PanDA monitor 
         * 1.2 M
   * Data Transfer statistics for last week
      * Data transfer rate was around 350~550TB/day at BNL T1.
