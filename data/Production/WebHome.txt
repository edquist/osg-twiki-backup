---+!! OSG Production
---+++ _OSG Production is the culmination of all the OSG efforts including testing, integration, services, support, operations, hardware, software, and security -- VO's get their jobs run, data analyzed, and in so doing enable the advancement of science._

 As the [[http://display.grid.iu.edu][DOE Display]] and Production Database show: 
   * OSG routinely runs 4+ Million Jobs and delivers 6+ Million cpu-hours every week.
   * A petabyte of data is transferred approximately every 1.5 days.
