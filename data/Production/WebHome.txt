---+!! OSG Production
---+++ _OSG Production is the culmination of all the OSG efforts including testing, integration, services, support, operations, hardware, software, and security -- when VO's get their jobs run, and thereby enable the advancement of science._

 As the [[http://display.grid.iu.edu][DOE Display]] shows: 
   * OSG routinely runs over 2 Million Jobs with more than 5 Million cpu-hours every week.
   * A petabyte of data is transferred approximately every 3 days.
