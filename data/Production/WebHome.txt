---+!! OSG Production
---+++ _OSG Production is the culmination of many OSG efforts including testing, integration, services, support, operations, hardware, software, and security -- VO's get their jobs run, their data analyzed, and in so doing enable the advancement of science._
%DOC_STATUS_TABLE%
 As the [[http://display.grid.iu.edu][DOE Display]] and Production Database show: 
   * OSG routinely runs 4+ Million Jobs and delivers 13+ Million cpu-hours every week.
   * A petabyte of data is transferred on average every day.
