---++ Background
The next OSG site administrator's workshop will be held at the Vanderbilt University, August 10, 11 2009 in occasion of CMS Tier 3 workshop.  
The focus will be the upgrading of OSG CE's and SE's with the OSG 1.2 production release.   The focus is on *practical*, hands-on issues relating to OSG site administration.
   * Meeting homepage in OSG Indico system is [[http://indico.fnal.gov/conferenceTimeTable.py?confId=3429][here]]
   * Timetable [[http://indico.fnal.gov/conferenceTimeTable.py?confId=3429][agenda]]
