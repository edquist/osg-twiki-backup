---++ Special topic: SVOPME policy work presentation - Nanbor Wang, !TechX
SVOPME is a tool for !VOs and sites that makes it easy to handle complex internal organizational structures and their privilege mappings at sites. The tool was developed in collaboration with !TechX as part of an SBIR. 
Today's presentation is targeted for VO and resource administrators with details about existing deployments.
   * Talk in PDF format: [[%ATTACHURL%/SVOPME-Introduction-Site.pdf][SVOPME-Introduction-Site.pdf]]

---++ Cluster survey summary
A survey has been submitted to OSG site administrators.
This survey is aimed to understand the technologies used by OSG sites at the cluster level, specifically to manage the hosts, the jobs, to replicate system files and to share files. It will be useful information to share within OSG and help in providing recommendations for new sites, specially Tier 3s.
   * The survey URL is: http://www.surveymonkey.com/s/8MLFZ56
   * Preliminary results: [[%ATTACHURL%/SurveySummary_12092010_3.pdf][SurveySummary_12092010_3.pdf]]
