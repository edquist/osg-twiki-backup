---++ Introduction
The Site Coordination coordinates and facilitates the deployment OSG software to OSG sites and provides support and training to the OSG site administrators community.
The [[Trash/Trash/Integration.WebHome][Integration]] and [[Trash/SiteCoordination.WebHome][SiteCoordination]] are part of the OSG Facility Project. See Trash/Trash/Integration.ProjectPlanning for more information.


---++ Chat
   * There is a Campfire Web chat that can be used for discussions or troubleshooting sessions. There are no regular office hours as of December 2012. See ChatCalendar 

---++ Adobe Connect Pro
