---++ Introduction

The Site Coordination coordinates and facilitates the deployment OSG software to OSG sites and provides support and training to the OSG site administrators community.
The [[Integration.WebHome][Integration]] and [[SiteCoordination.WebHome][SiteCoordination]] are part of the OSG Facility Project. See Integration.ProjectPlanning for more information.
