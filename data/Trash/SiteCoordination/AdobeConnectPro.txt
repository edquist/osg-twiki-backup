---++ Conference Attendees
Anyone can go to the URL of an Adobe Connect meeting from any browser with Flash and attend the meeting.
Login is required only to schedule a new meeting or to be the initial moderator of a meeting.
The moderators of the meeting can grant presenter or moderator privilege to other attendees.
This [[http://www.adobe.com/go/connectpro_overview][link]] contains an overview of how the system works. You can test attending a meeting
[[http://osg.acrobat.com/common/help/en/support/meeting_test.htm][here]].

---++ Adobe Connect Users
   * We will be using Adobe Connect to host teleconferences in OSG.  A special meeting URL will be advertised for meetings organized with Adobe Connect.
   * You do not need a license or any special software to connect to a meeting.  All that is needed is a web browser and the Adobe Flash Player.
   * If you need to present a talk in a meeting, then you will need a license as *Presenter*.  Licenses can be assigned temporarily during a meeting by the meeting *Host*.
   * Licenses can also be assigned  indefinitely by the account *Administrator* (we have a limited number available).  If you think you need a license, please contact Marco Mambelli (marco@hep.uchicago.edu) who is the manager for the OSG-University of Chicago Adobe Connect Pro account.

---++ Adobe Connect Administrators
   * Getting started: https://admin.adobe.acrobat.com/_a227210/vqs-gettingstartedmeeting/
   * If you plan to schedule a meeting you need a license as a *Host*, i.e., an account. 
   * If you have a *Host* account you can log-in at http://admin.acrobat.com  and schedule/manage meetings
