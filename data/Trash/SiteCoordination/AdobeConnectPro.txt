---++Adobe Acrobat Connect Pro
With Adobe® Acrobat® Connect™ Pro software, you can create instant, collaborative meetings with just a web browser and the Adobe Flash® Player.
   * Getting started: https://admin.adobe.acrobat.com/_a227210/vqs-gettingstartedmeeting/

---++Use
There is no need of license to connect to a conference/training.

If you plan to schedule a meeting you need a license as _Host_. If you need to present in a Meeting you need a license as _Presenter_.

Licenses can be assigned temporarily during a meeting by the meeting _Host_ or indefinitely by an account _Administrator_.  If you need one, please contact Marco (marco@hep.uchicago.edu), account manaber for the OSG/University of Chicago. 

If you have an account you can log-in at http://admin.acrobat.com  to manage the meetings
