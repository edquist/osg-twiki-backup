---++ Report about the grid effort done in Brazil (Rogerio L. Iope)
Rogerio L. Iope of the São Paulo State University presented the status of the Grid in the São Paulo state (south west of Brazil).
There are several resources in different facilities sparse within the state, all belonging to the São Paulo State University, with several CEs and SEs, all using OSG middleware. Other universities are in the process of joining.
For more details you can [[%ATTACHURL%/GridUnesp_RLIope_OSG_sites_coord_Feb_2011.pdf][check the presentation (GridUnesp_RLIope_OSG_sites_coord_Feb_2011.pdf)]] or look at the recordings of the session.
