---+++ Tracking documents for which you have some responsibility
There are a set of TWiki variables (command like) that will create tables on your home page (typically !Main.FirstLast) that will list documents for which you are the Owner, Reviewer, or Tester.  Variables also exist for Area owners of documentation.
---++++ Your home page
The table below lists the commands you can place on your home page to create various tables that may be of use.  Each of these have columns for the document name (link), current status, names of people who are associated, and last person to modify.
| *Table* | *Command* | *Variable Used* |
| Documents you Own | !%xxx% | Set !%NAME% to your TWiki id |
A template that you may copy and paste into your home page is DocHomePageTemplate.
---++++ For management of a document Area
Additional commands are available to generate tables of documents by area or all documents.  The tables generated by these commands also have columns for the document name (link), current status, names of people who are associated, and last person to modify.
| *Table* | *Command* | *Variable Used* |
| Documents in an Area | !%yyy% | Set !%AREA% to the desired area |
