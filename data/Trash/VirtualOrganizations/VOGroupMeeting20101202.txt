---++ SVOPME policy work presentation - Nanbor Wang, !TechX

SVOPME is a tool for !VOs and sites that makes it easy to handle complex internal organizational structures and their privilege mappings at sites. The tool was developed in collaboration with !TechX as part of an SBIR. 

!TechX is interesting in finding a VO that wants to try out these tools.
