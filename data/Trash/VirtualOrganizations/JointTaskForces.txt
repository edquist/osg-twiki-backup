---+ *Joint !TaskForces*

%TABLE{ tablewidth="700" columnwidths="100%" cellpadding="4" dataalign="left" tablerules="all" tableborder="4" databg="#FFFFFF"}%
| <font size="+0"> With guidance from OSG Executive Director, *Joint Task Forces* are planned and executed to enable wide-range technical and procedural matters. <br><br> %ICON{more}%  Organizational framework of each !TaskForce is coordinated by rallying concerted effort and focused drive from consortium stakeholders, as well as, from multiple OSG groups. <br> %ICON{more}%  Investment of focus is threefold: technological strategy, sociological organization, disciplined execution. <br> %ICON{more}%  Due deliberation is performed to enable the stakeholder to sustain the momentum, beyond a successful closure of !TaskForce, to maximize the long-term residual impact. </font> |

| ALICE-OSG !TaskForce | Active | Working to enable ALICE's specialized !AliEn framework and ALICE production on OSG Facility. |
| nanoHUB-OSG !TaskForce | Active | Investigating ways to make workflow improvements, to enable nanoHUB science production to high job volume at high efficiency. |
| Geant4-OSG !TaskForce | Active | Working to enable Geant4's Regression Testing production runs on OSG Facility. |
