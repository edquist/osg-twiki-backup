---+ Summary

With input from Executive Team, *Joint Task Forces* are planned and executed to enable wide-range technical and procedural matters by rallying concerted effort from VOs as well as from OSG groups. 

   * D0-OSG Taskforce led to a significant improvement in overall D0 monte-carlo event production on OSG. 
   * SBGrid-OSG Taskforce worked closely together to enable SBGrid resource infrastructure and to evolve design and implementation of the SBGrid Molecular Replacement science application, to strengthen each of these to a production-ready level. 
   * ALICE-OSG Taskforce is working to enable ALICE's specialized !AliEn framework and ALICE production on OSG Facility.
   * nanoHUB-OSG Taskforce is investigating ways to make workflow improvements, to enable nanoHUB science production to high job volume at high efficiency.
   * Geant4-OSG Taskforce is working to enable Geant4's Regression Testing production runs on OSG Facility.
