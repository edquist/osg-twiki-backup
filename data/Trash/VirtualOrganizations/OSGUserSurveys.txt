---++ Goal
---+++ *How does this work?*

This is a <u>%MAROON%goal-oriented%ENDCOLOR%</u> and quarterly exercise, intended <u>%MAROON%to engage in a direct dialog with VOs%ENDCOLOR%</u> which derive science benefits from use of OSG resources. We provide each VO with a dedicated page complete with monthly usage plots for the past quarter and 3 survey questions. Usage plots are based on daily usage by site for a VO, as reported by Gratia accounting. After each VO has answered the questions, we convey the results to the Executive Team. In response to your feedback, if any action is needed to improve your satisfaction, appropriate measures will be taken as soon as possible. <u>%MAROON%This is primarily for your, the VO's benefit!%ENDCOLOR%</u> We thank you in advance for taking out time to complete the survey. 

---+++ *What do we expect you to do?*

Please note the 'VO Participation table' below, and visit the twiki page of VO you represent. Analyze the usage plots we have provided and fill-in answers to the questions on your VO's own twiki page. You can fill-in by using the 'Edit' button beneath the 'Survey Answers table' for this quarter. Use '%MAROON%br%ENDCOLOR%' with angle brackets to add line breaks if needed. If everything is in order, anticipated time needed to complete the survey is only a handful of minutes.
