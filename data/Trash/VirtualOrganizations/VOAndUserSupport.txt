---+++ %BLACK% *Meetings* %ENDCOLOR%

The VO Forum is a periodic phone call with the primary purpose of assisting VOs and User Groups in efficiently accomplishing their science.  Via these, calls we work to understand the computation goals of the community, issues, and challenges and develop paths for resolving issues and providing consulting help for the user community.                                                                         
 [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/VirtualOrganizations/Meetings][These meeting are typically held every other week at 1:30pm central time.]]    

The User Support Group holds bi-weekly team meetings to review progress on the work program described below; these are typically every other Tuesday at 1pm central and meeting notices are sent out to the team.
