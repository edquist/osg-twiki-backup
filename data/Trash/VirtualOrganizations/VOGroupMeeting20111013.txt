---+++ !SuperB - Steffen Luitz
   * [[%ATTACHURL%/OSG-forum-SuperB-Luitz.pdf][SuperB VO presentation at VO Forum on Oct 13, 2011]]

   * New accelerator to be constructed in Frascati (Rome)
   * Will re-use sections of !BaBar detector with several new components
   * Expect to be operational in the 2016-2017 timeframe
   * Computing model evolved from !BaBar
   * ~10 data centers planned, most in Europe; when at production volumes, expect about 10-20 PBytes/ year.  Flat topology vs a tiered hierarchy
   * Goal is a grid based computing environment
   * Today ITFN-CNAF is the the central site and they submit to various sites from there; running simulation
   * For analysis, expect to have about 10 sites; possibly SLAC, based on funding outcomes.
