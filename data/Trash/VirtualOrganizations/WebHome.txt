---++ Mission Statement

<table width="100%"><tr><td valign="top"></td><td valign="middle" width="50%">
OSG User Group serves as liaison between <font color=#003399><b>Science Communities</b></font>, commonly structured as Virtual Organizations (VOs), who use the OSG grid cyber-infrastructure and the OSG Executive Board. User Group works with the Middleware, Operations, Applications, Security, Storage, Sites coordination groups to address requirements, feedback, issues and roadmaps.

In this capacity, User Group aims to understand the <i>desiderata</i> - expectations and operational aspects - of running VO applications on OSG. As need arises, the group also provides accelerated support to enable VOs' science milestones, serves as an ad-hoc platform for communication across [[http://www.opensciencegrid.org/VO_List][all the VOs]], and represents Users' requirements and interests to fulfill [[http://www.opensciencegrid.org/About/Learn_About_Us/Our_Mission][OSG's mission]].

---++ Activities & Focus Areas

%ICON{more}% <font color=#0066FF><b>General </b></font>
   * Work with VOs on current problems, especially resource utilization as needed for specific runs.
   * VOs' management of Job submission and execution, Data movement and storage, Security and Policy.

%ICON{more}% <font color=#0066FF><b>VO Planning and Requirements towards Future Releases of OSG </b></font>
   * [[VOApplicationsRequirements][OSG 1.0/1.2 Planning: Requirements]] set forth by VOs.
   * Facilitating VOs' requirements to serve as direct input to OSG software engineering plans.
   * In partnership with OSG Project Management and Middleware/VDT team.

%ICON{more}% <font color=#0066FF><b>VO Validation of Sites on Integration Testbed </b></font>
   * VO application validation of ITB to enable official releases of OSG, in close-knit partnership with OSG Integration.
   * Archive: [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Integration/ITB090/SiteValidationTable#VO_validation][ITB 0.9.x %ICON{external}% OSG 1.0]] , [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/ReleaseDocumentation/SiteValidationTable#VO_validation][ITB 0.7.x %ICON{external}% OSG 0.8]] ,  [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Integration/SiteValidationTableITB052][ITB 0.5.x %ICON{external}% OSG 0.6]] .

%ICON{more}% <font color=#0066FF><b>Development of a Site Testing Package for use by VOs </b></font>
   * Development of a [[OsgVoSiteTests][Extensible VO-centric Site Testing Package]] - to be of high-utility for all VOs. 
   * Site Testing Package is ready for adoption and usage by VOs, alongside OSG 1.0.
   * [[OsgVoSiteTests#Overview][Overview]] | [[OsgVoSiteTests#Download_Source][Download]] | [[OsgVoSiteTests#Getting_Started][Use]] | [[mailto:osg-user-group?subject=osg-vo-tests][Give feedback]]

%ICON{more}% <font color=#0066FF><b>OSG VO </b></font>
   * Coordination of OSG VO.
   * Platform for transition of new Science communities to develop into established OSG Users and VOs.
   * In partnership with, and bootstrapping from, OSG Engagement and OSG Education Outreach Training (EOT).

%ICON{more}% <font color=#0066FF><b>Enabling MPI Resource Advertisement, Discovery, and Usage </b></font>
   * Work is in progress with OSG Engagement and !ReSS teams, MPI Resource providers, OSG Operations (GOC).

%ICON{more}% <font color=#0066FF><b>Security Policies and Procedures related to VOs </b></font>
   * Partnership with OSG Security Office to enable matters related to VOs.

%ICON{more}% <font color=#0066FF><b>Accelerated Drive to enable Science </b></font>
   * Targeted work to accelerate OSG's support for specific VOs who are producing Science results. | [[AcceleratedDrive][Draft]]
   * Focus is on value-addition in Science, beyond LHC, and spans across broad areas within OSG technology lifecycle.
   * Ensuring technology's readiness, availability, site adoption, deployment, user adoption, operations, troubleshooting.
   * In partnership with OSG Project Management, OSG Sites including Validation/Integration/Production, OSG Troubleshooting, OSG Operations (GOC), Middleware/VDT including external technical projects.

%ICON{more}% <font color=#0066FF><b>Evolving Workload Management capabilities of OSG</b></font>
   * Moving towards more user-friendly and efficient access of VOs to computational resources across multiple sites.
   * Ensuring adequate security and interoperability in workload management with peer Grids.

%ICON{more}% <font color=#0066FF><b>Evolving and Strengthening the Data-Grid capabilities of OSG </b></font>
   * Partnership with OSG Storage to gradually enable opportunistic usage of deployed storage for all VOs. 

%ICON{more}% <font color=#0066FF><b>OSG Biannual VO Usage Feedback and Direct Dialog </b></font>
   * Forthcoming, Direct Dialog for Quarter 2 '08. | [[Draft][Draft in preparation]]
   * OSG User satisfaction surveys, introduced in Quarter 3 '07. | Archive: [[OSGUserSurveys][Quarter 3 '07]]

---++ Mailing Lists & News

The <u>OSG Users list</u> (=osg-users at opensciencegrid dot org=), [[http://listserv.fnal.gov/archives/osg-users.html][archived here]], consists of representatives of all officially registered stakeholding VOs in the OSG, and is the primary medium for circulation of important announcements and news items. This list also serves as a general VO/user discussion forum. Representatives of newly formed VOs are strongly encouraged to [[http://listserv.fnal.gov/scripts/wa.exe?SUBED1=osg-users&A=1][subscribe to the list]]. A very small subset of announcements is occasionally also posted on the [[UserGroupNewsItems][news page]].

The <u>OSG User Group Coordination list</u> (=osg-user-group at opensciencegrid dot org=) consists of the OSG User Group Coordinators, OSG Applications Coordinators, and the OSG Executive Director. A VO interested in communicating with the coordination team can feel free to [[mailto:OSG-USER-GROUP@OPENSCIENCEGRID.ORG][send an email to this list]] anytime.
