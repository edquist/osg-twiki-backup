---++ Overview

The User Support group aims to provide help and information to VOs and application owners; and to represent the users' requirements and interests to OSG.

---++ Meetings

[[UserSupportMeetings][Meetings]] take place every week on Thursday at 11am.

General attendance of users with issues to discuss is encouraged: please see our [[UserSupportMeetings][meetings]] page for coordinates and agendas of past and future meetings.
d29 7
---++ Current Activities

   * Work with VOs on current problems, especially resource utilization as needed for specific runs.
   * Introducing [[OSGUserSurveys][OSG User satisfaction surveys]] for Quarter 2 '07 (03/15 - 06/15). 
   * VO application validation of ITB 0.7.x towards release of OSG 0.8.0.
   * An extensible [[OsgVoSiteTests][VO-centric site testing package]].
