---++ Joining the MARIACHI VO

All participants in MARIACHI are eligible for membership in the MARIACHI Virtual Organization. 

   1 Before applying, read and understand the [[http://osg-docdb.opensciencegrid.org/cgi-bin/ShowDocument?docid=86][OSG Acceptable Use Policy]] 
   1 To request membership, connect to the [[https://www-mariachi.physics.sunysb.edu:8443/voms/mariachi/webui/request/user/create][MARIACHI VOMS (Virtual Organization Management Server]] from a browser that has your grid certificate. Provide the requested information, and your application will be handled. 

If you have problems, please e-mail Main.JohnHover
