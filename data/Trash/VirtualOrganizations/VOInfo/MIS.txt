---+ !MIS VO
---++ Introduction
This document details basic information about the !MIS VO for participants and inquirers as well as the configuration information necessary for granting the !MIS VO access to a site.

---++ !MIS General Information
!MIS, which stands for Monitoring Information Systems, is a VO expressly created as a vessel for !OSG Operations infrastructural services, such as operational troubleshooting and security firedrills. !MIS replaced and refined the mission of the former !iVDGL VO. The Security team and the Grid Operations Center are the primary stakeholders in this VO.

---++ !MIS Privilege Information
This section describes the !MIS VO privilege information needed to allow the services within the !MIS VO access to a particular site. Included here is the GUMS and VOMS information that must be in place.

---+++ !MIS VO Usage Rules

The purpose of the !MIS VO is to monitor the OSG infastructure. The !MIS VO is for production level services which have been properly tested and proven stable in the !OSG-ITB and will not be opened to developmental monitoring services.

   * OSG Usage Policy - !MIS service administrators agree to the OSG Usage Policy located here.
   * Proper Usage - Services allowed into the !MIS VO will use the it only for monitoring the OSG-wide infastructure.
   * Action Within the Scope of !MIS - Services will not use the !MIS VO to run VO, site, or application specific jobs.
   * Regard for Security - Service administrators agree to keep credentials in a secured area.
   * Incident Reporting - If a machine containing the credentials for a service is compromised the administator of that service agrees to contact the !OSG-GOC at goc@opensciencegrid.org
