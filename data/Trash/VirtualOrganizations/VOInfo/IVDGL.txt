---+iVDGL VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo Page

For more information on the iVDGL Virtual Organization, please go to [[http://www.ivdgl.org][iVDGL Home Page]].

---++Privilege Information

---+++Introduction

This page describe the iVDGL VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo privilege information needed to allow people and services within the iVDGL VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo access to a particular site. Included here is the GUMS and VirtualOrganizations/VOInfoMS information that must be in place. 

---+++Requesting Membership

To request membership in the iVDGL VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo please complete the following steps:

   * Go [[https://grid03.uits.indiana.edu:8443/edg-voms-admin/vos/webui/UserRequest/create][here]] and complete the form. NOTE: Your DOE cert must be loaded into the browser you are using to make the request.
   * Complete the Institute and Phone Number fields. If the Name feilds are not completed you do not have your cert loaded into your browser correctly.
   * Enter "iVDGL" in the comment field.
   * Read the iVDGL Usage Rules below and click "I have read and agree to the VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo's Usage Rules".         
   * You will receive an email confirmation, follow the link in the email.
   * Once this is done the VirtualOrganizations/VOInfoMS administrator will confirm your membership with the appropiate VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo representative and let you know when you have been added. 

---+++Usage Rules

The iVDGL VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo's primary purpose is the development of computer science applications on the OSG infastructure. Addition into the iVDGL VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo requires a user to agree to the following Usage Rules.

   * OSG Usage Policy - Users agree to the OSG Usage Policy located here.
   * Proper Usage - Users will use the iVDGL VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo for its intended purpose of developing and running grid based applications on the OSG infastructure.
   * Action Within the Scope of iVDGL - Users will not use the iVDGL VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo to run jobs related to other OSG VOs.
   * Malicious Actions & Access - Users agree to use the iVDGL VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo and OSG resources for lawful actions and will not share credentials with other individuals.
   * Regard for Security - Users agree to keep systems which contain user credentials and VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo account information secure.
   * Membership in an iVDGL Institution - Only members of institutions named in the iVDGL grant will be accepted into the iVDGL VO.
   * Incident Reporting - If a machine containing a users credentials is compromised the user agrees to immediately contact the OSG-GOC at goc@opensciencegrid.org
