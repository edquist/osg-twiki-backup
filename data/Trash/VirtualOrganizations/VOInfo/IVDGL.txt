---+iVDGL VO Page

For more information on the iVDGL Virtual Organization, please go to [[http://www.ivdgl.org][iVDGL Home Page]].

---++Privilege Information

---+++Introduction

This page describe the iVDGL VO privilege information needed to allow people and services within the iVDGL VO access to a particular site. Included here is the GUMS and VOMS information that must be in place. 

---+++Requesting Membership

To request membership in the iVDGL VO please complete the following steps:

	* Go [[https://grid03.uits.indiana.edu:8443/edg-voms-admin/vos/webui/UserRequest/create][here]] and complete the form. NOTE: Your DOE cert must be loaded into the browser you are using to make the request.
	* Complete the Institute and Phone Number fields. If the Name feilds are not completed you do not have your cert loaded into your browser correctly.
	* Enter "iVDGL" in the comment field.
	* Read the iVDGL Usage Rules below and click "I have read and agree to the VO's Usage Rules".			
	* You will receive an email confirmation, follow the link in the email.
	* Once this is done the VOMS administrator will confirm your membership with the appropiate VO representative and let you know when you have been added. 

---+++Usage Rules

The iVDGL VO's primary purpose is the development of computer science applications on the OSG infastructure. Addition into the iVDGL VO requires a user to agree to the following Usage Rules.

	* OSG Usage Policy - Users agree to the OSG Usage Policy located here.
	* Proper Usage - Users will use the iVDGL VO for its intended purpose of developing and running grid based applications on the OSG infastructure.
	* Action Within the Scope of iVDGL - Users will not use the iVDGL VO to run jobs related to other OSG VOs.
	* Malicious Actions & Access - Users agree to use the iVDGL VO and OSG resources for lawful actions and will not share credentials with other individuals.
	* Regard for Security - Users agree to keep systems which contain user credentials and VO account information secure.
	* Membership in an iVDGL Institution - Only members of institutions named in the iVDGL grant will be accepted into the iVDGL VO.
	* Incident Reporting - If a machine containing a users credentials is compromised the user agrees to immediately contact the OSG-GOC at goc@opensciencegrid.org
