---+ !OSG VO Privilege Information
---+++ Introduction

This section describes the !OSG VO privilege information needed to allow the services within the !OSG VO access to a particular site. Included here is the GUMS and VOMS information that must be in place.

---+++ Requesting !OSG VO Membership

To request membership in the !OSG VO please complete the following steps:

   * Go [[https://grid03.uits.indiana.edu:8443/edg-voms-admin/vos/webui/UserRequest/create][here]] and complete the form. NOTE: Your !DOE cert must be loaded into the browser you are using to make the request.
   * Complete the Institute and Phone Number fields. If the Name feilds are not completed you do not have your cert loaded into your browser correctly.
   * Enter "OSG" in the comment field.
   * Read the !OSG Usage Rules below and click "I have read and agree to the VO's Usage Rules".
   * You will receive an email confirmation, follow the link in the email.
   * Once this is done the !VOMS administrator will confirm your membership with the appropiate VO re presentative and let you know when you have been added. 

---+++ !OSG VO Usage Rules

The purpose of the !OSG VO is to provide standard services to experiments that do not have the resources to provide thier own.

   * !OSG Usage Policy - !OSG VO users agree to the !OSG Usage Policy located here.
   * Action Within the Scope of !OSG - Users will not use the !OSG VO to run VO applications that are directly related to an existing VO.
   * Incident Reporting - If a machine containing the credentials for a user is compromised the administator of that service agrees to contact the !OSG-GOC at goc@opensciencegrid.org
