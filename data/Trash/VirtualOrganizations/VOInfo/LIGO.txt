---++ Joining the !LIGO VO
To become a member of the !LIGO VO, please do the following:
   * [[http://www.lsc-group.phys.uwm.edu/lscdatagrid/doc/certificates.html][Obtain]] a certificate, and then
   * [[https://ligo-web.aset.psu.edu/account.shtml][Request]] an account

---++ !LIGO Requirements
   * site-verify script with updates to check for VDS during !OSG installation.
   * VORS DB for information gathering via the utility vds-get-sites.

---++ !LIGO resources on !OSG
   * !UWMilwaukee (production cluster)
      gatekeeper: nest.phys.uwm.edu
      scheduler: Condor

   * !OSG_LIGO_PSU (production cluster)
      gatekeeper: grid3.aset.psu.edu
      scheduler: PBS

   * !OSG_ITB_PSU (testbed cluster)
      gatekeeper: ligo-db2.aset.psu.edu
      scheduler: PBS

   * !LIGO-CIT-ITB (testbed cluster)
      gatekeeper: osg-itb.ligo.caltech.edu
      scheduler: Condor

---++ !LIGO Support Center
   The !LIGO support center can be reached by emailing *rt-osg __at__ ligo-mon.aset.psu.edu* or the !OSG GOC. Please DO NOT contact the sites directly.
