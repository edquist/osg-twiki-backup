---+ Groups and Privileges in the ILC VO

These are the accounts and privileges for full site support for the ILC VO:

| *Account* | *Group* | *Additional Info* |
| ilcacc | /ilc/accelerator | for international linear collider simulation studies |
| ilcdet | /ilc/detector | for general ILC detector simulation studies |
| ilc4c | /ilc/ilc4c | for [[http://www.4thconcept.org/][4th]] concept detector studies |
| ilddet | /ilc/ild | for [[http://www-flc.desy.de/ild/index.php][ILD]] detector studies |
| siddet | /ilc/sid | for [[http://silicondetector.org/][SiD]] detector studies |

Users are encouraged to use the specific detector groups rather than /ilc/detector.  If you register in the [[https://voms.fnal.gov:8443/vomrs/ilc/vomrs][ILC VirtualOrganizations/VOInfoMRS]], you will see this group structure. 

Definitions for these accounts and roles are part of the standard !OSG VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo Package gums.template.
