---++ Joining the <noautolink>LIGO</noautolink> VO
   Membership to the <noautolink>LIGO</noautolink> VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo is restricted to active members of the <noautolink>LIGO</noautolink> Scientific Collaboration. If you are currently a member of the LSC, you need to obtain a Grid certificate and request cluster resource access. Please follow these instructions to become a member of the <noautolink>LIGO</noautolink> VO:
   * [[http://www.lsc-group.phys.uwm.edu/lscdatagrid/doc/certificates.html][Obtain]] a Grid certificate, and then
   * [[https://ligo-web.aset.psu.edu/account.shtml][Request]] an account for access to <noautolink>LIGO</noautolink> and <noautolink>OSG</noautolink> resources
