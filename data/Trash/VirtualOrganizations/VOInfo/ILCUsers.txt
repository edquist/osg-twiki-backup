---++ How to register with the !ILC VO

The !ILC VO uses the [[https://voms.fnal.gov:8443/vomrs/ilc/vomrs][VOMRS software]], to manage registration with the VO. 
You may register with either a x509 Kerberos CA user certificate or a DOE Grids user certificate.  To register, go to the [[https://voms.fnal.gov:8443/vomrs/ilc/vomrs][ILC instance of VOMRS]].  More information is available at [[http://ilc.fnal.gov/detector/rd/physics/technical/resources/grid.shtml][VO registration]].

Please choose the appropriate group: 

| *Account* | *Group* | *Additional Info* |
| ilcacc | /ilc/accelerator | for international linear collider simulation studies |
| ilcdet | /ilc/detector | for general !ILC detector simulation studies |
| ilc4c | /ilc/ilc4c | for [[http://www.4thconcept.org/][4th]] concept detector studies |
| ilddet | /ilc/ild | for [[http://www-flc.desy.de/ild/index.php][ILD]] detector studies |
| siddet | /ilc/sid | for [[http://silicondetector.org/][SiD]] detector studies |

Users are encouraged to use the specific detector groups rather than /ilc/detector.  

---++ Policies

Your use of the Open Science Grid (!OSG) is subject to your acceptance of the [[http://osg-docdb.opensciencegrid.org/cgi-bin/ShowDocument?docid=86][OSG User Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)]] and the [[http://ilcdoc.linearcollider.org/getfile.py?docid=133&name=ILC-UsersAgreement&format=pdf&version=1][!ILC VO rules]].

The !ILC VO shares machines with !FermiGrid, so much of the same documentation applies.
