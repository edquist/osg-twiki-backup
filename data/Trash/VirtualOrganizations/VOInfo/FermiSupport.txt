---+ User Support for the fermilab VO
The fermilab VO uses the Fermilab Computing Division helpdesk facility for support issues.

Email your support issue to  _helpdesk-admin@fnal.gov_.  In the *subject line*, indicate that this relates to the 
   * fermilab VO,
   * !FermiGrid, or
   * !OSG.

The fermilab VO support staff gets these tickets assigned through the Fermilab standard Remedy system.

For security issues, contact the Computer security at _computer_security@fnal.gov_.
