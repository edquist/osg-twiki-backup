Unfamiliar with Virtual Organizations (VOs) and all they bring with them? Read [[Documentation/VirtualOrganizations/VOInfoAlphabetSoup][VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo Alphabet Soup]].

Welcome to the home of <b>%WIKITOOLNAME%.%WEB%</b>. This is a web-based collaboration area for each of the !OSG Virtual Organizations to provide its own VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo-specific information to its members.  Create pages to record your VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo-specific practices, configurations, etc. for users and admins.  Please organize the information according to the given links.  Record your experiences, your success/failure rate at each !OSG site, and other information that your members and/or other VOs may find useful. <!-- In that ideal future, we will then provide an easy to navigate way for site admins to see their score, and other VOs to see which sites are worthwhile trying out, and how to go about it.-->

#VeeO
   $ VO: The VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo name; include overview of VO, OSG contacts for VO, link to VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo's home page, intro to VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo's implementation of OSG software, links to any news items.
#UtilStat
   $ Util Status: Utilization status for VO
#UserInfo
   $ User info: Include/link to info such as how to join VO, what CA(s) to use, how to select resources, how to use application, and your VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo's resource access policies
#AppL
   $ Application: Include/link to all required information for application admins
#PriVlg
   $ Privileges: Include/link to VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo's defnition of member groups and roles, and their associated privileges.
#SuccFail
   $ Success/Failure: Record your VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo's success/failure rate at each site, plus any comments, concerns, etc.
#InformalComments
   $ Informal Comments: Record any "off the record" information for your colleagues.
