---++ Overview

The SBGrid VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo is based at Harvard Medical School in Boston, MA. We're a a computing collaboration of several dozen structural biology (X-ray crystallography, NMR and electron microscopy) laboratories across the country. 

For more information, visit [[http://sbgrid.org]].
