---+++ %RED% *Form for new Researchers who want to use the OSG VO for their computation* %ENDCOLOR%

New researchers are asked to register their projects in the OSG VO by completing the attached form.  This form covers

   * PI-Last-Name
   * PI-First-Name
   * Email of the PI
   * Organization/University
   * Department 
   * Join-date 
   * Field-of-Science	
   * !ProjectName < provided by OSG; a string that uniquely identifies this project >
   * Sponsor = < provided by OSG; typically this is the name of a VO or Campus Grid >
   * OSG Sponsor Contact = < provided by OSG; the name of the OSG staff member supporting this PI >
   * Abstract-of-Work < a short description of the research; < 150 words >
   * Match-for-OSG < provide a short description of why you think your compuations is a good match for OSG >

All new researchers are asked to complete the Project Registration Form attached below; please name your file using the following syntax:  "Lastname-Firstname@Institution".  Please email the completed form to user-support@opensciencegrid.org .
