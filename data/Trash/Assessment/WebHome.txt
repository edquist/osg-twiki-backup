---++ Introduction
%DOC_STATUS_TABLE%
The Assessment activity has been formed to track overall progress of various OSG tasks and activities against strategic goals. 
This activity works with the Technical Area Leads and Executive Team to define, measure, and publish agreed upon metrics and provide a framework in which these can be reported and understood.

This activity restarted with new people in FY2012 after a hiatus. It is a work in progress.
