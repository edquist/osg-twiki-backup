---+ Install a certificate into a web browser

   1. After download the certificate, start your browser

   1. Open your Certificates Manager
       <p> %ATTACHURL%/Certificates_Manager.png</p>
   1. Click "Import" and select the certificate just downloaded. 
      <p> %ATTACHURL%/Certificate_Import.png  </p>
   1. After select the certificate, this window will be shown
      <p> %ATTACHURL%/Certificate_Added.png  </p>
