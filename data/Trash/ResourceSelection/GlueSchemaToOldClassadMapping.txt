   * <b>Glue Schema to Old Classad Mapping</b> : This document describes a mapping between the GLUE schema and old !ClassAd format. The old !ClassAd representation of resource characteristics can be used in outof- the-box resource selection technologies, such as the Condor Matchmaking Service. This mapping has been written focussing on the problem of resource selection and is presented as a series of rules with examples.
      * [[http://glueschema.forge.cnaf.infn.it/SpecV13/OldClassAd][Glue Schema v1.3 to old classad mapping]]
