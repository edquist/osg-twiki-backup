This document describes a mapping between the GLUE schema v1.2 and old ClassAd format. The old ClassAd representation of resource characteristics can be used in outof- the-box resource selection technologies, such as the Condor Matchmaking Service. This mapping has been written focussing on the problem of resource selection and is presented as a series of rules with examples.
