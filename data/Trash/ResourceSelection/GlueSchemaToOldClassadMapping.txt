   * *Glue Schema to Old Classad Mapping* : This document describes a mapping between the GLUE schema and old !ClassAd format. The old !ClassAd representation of resource characteristics can be used in outof- the-box resource selection technologies, such as the Condor Matchmaking Service. This mapping has been written focussing on the problem of resource selection and is presented as a series of rules with examples. 
      * [[%ATTACHURL%/glue-v1.3-oldclassad-mapping-v1.1.pdf][Glue Schema v1.3 to old classad - mapping v1.1]]
