---+ Useful Links for CEMon Validation                              
   * CEMon reports to BDII consumer, sending event in RAW format     
      * [[http://is.grid.iu.edu/cgi-bin/status.cgi][OSG installation]]
      * [[http://is-itb2.grid.iu.edu/cgi-bin/status.cgi][ITB installation]]

      * [[http://osg-ress-1.fnal.gov:8080/ReSS/ReSS-prd-ClassadValidity.html][OSG installation - classad validation]]
      * [[http://osg-ress-1.fnal.gov:8080/ReSS/ReSS-int-History.html][ITB installation - IG]]
      * [[http://osg-ress-1.fnal.gov:8080/ReSS/ReSS-int-ClassadValidity.html][ITB installation - classad validation]]
