<h2>Comments</h2>
In my opinion, the goal of (1) is to decide if there is enough interest to write a document about this. Within the context of ReSS (resource selection) for DZero, we have come up with a mapping that we have implemented as a "sensor" in CEMon. You can look at the email thread at http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/bin/view/ResourceSelection/CEMonCollaboration

The goal for (2) is to understand if the architecture of resource selection on OSG for DZero can be used for ATLAS as well. We can identify use cases and requirements, then discuss areas of work on the current architecture. The requirements on resource selection for DZero are at
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/bin/view/ResourceSelection/DZeroRequirements
