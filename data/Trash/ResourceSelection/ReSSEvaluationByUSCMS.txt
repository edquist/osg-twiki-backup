---+ !ReSS Evaluation by USCMS

USCMS has sponsored an evaluation of Workload Management Systems with the goal of selecting a technology appropriate for the USCMS requirements on computing in the US.

The evaluation shows that the !GlideIn Factory, enhanced with !ReSS for cluster selection, are suitable technologies for USCMS on the OSG.

   * [[https://indico.fnal.gov/contributionDisplay.py?contribId=65&sessionId=13&confId=468][Evaluation of Workload Management Systems for OSG]]: Talk at the OSG council meeting on Mar 07.
