The six metrics studied for year one were:

   1.  Number of compute resources passing site verification
      * Goal: Average over year > 35
      * Goal: Best month > 60
   1.  Number of OSG jobs per day (Goal: > 50,000)
   1.  Data moved between OSG storage resources (Goal: >100TB)
   1.  Percentage of support tickets with a solution accepted in 1 week (Goal: >50%)
   1.  Time taken to release a software patch into production infrastructure (Goal: >95%)
   1.  Number of VOs achieving 100% of throughput for science/research goals (Goal: 80%)
