The Joint Oversight Team gets what we call "Metrics Thumbnails" each month which shows, at a high level, how the CMS and ATLAS use OSG.  However, this data comes from disparate sources.  This page is an overview of what data we collect, and how to collect it.

---+++ Number of Jobs

Use this URL

[[http://t2.unl.edu/gratia/xml/osg_vo_count?starttime=2008-05-01%2000:00:00&endtime=2008-06-01%2000:00:00&vo=cms|atlas][http://t2.unl.edu/gratia/xml/osg_vo_count?starttime=2008-05-01 00:00:00&endtime=2008-06-01 00:00:00&vo=cms|atlas]]

Edit the month end and begin date in the above URL to reflect the current month.

---+++ Wall clock hours

Use this URL

[[http://t2.unl.edu/gratia/xml/osg_vo_hours?starttime=2008-05-01%2000:00:00&endtime=2008-06-01%2000:00:00&vo=cms|atlas][http://t2.unl.edu/gratia/xml/osg_vo_hours?starttime=2008-05-01 00:00:00&endtime=2008-06-01 00:00:00&vo=cms|atlas]]

Edit this month end and begin date in the above URL to reflect the current month.

---+++ 6-month wall clock hours average

Edit the above URLs to include the previous 6 months.
