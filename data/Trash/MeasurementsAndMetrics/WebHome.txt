The measurements and metrics web is the spot for coordinating related work on the OSG . Measurements and Metrics efforts are divided up into the following:
   * [[YearOneMetrics][Year One Metrics]] - Metrics work from OSG year one.  Work has been completed.
   * [[Measurements][Measurements]] - Measurements currently being worked on
   * [[YearTwoMetrics][Year Two Metrics]] - Metrics work for OSG year two.  This work is ongoing.
   * [[MMSandbox][Working Issues]] - In progress issues.
   * [[MMGIP][Generic Information Provider Extensions]] - Work needed with the GIP in order to appropriately mine it for useful data.
   * [[GratiaGraphs][Installing the Measurements and Metrics Graphs]] - How to install the graphing system for the Measurements and Metrics group.
   * [[MonthlyMetrics][Generating Monthly Metrics for the JOT]] - How to gather the information necessary for the monthly thumbnails.
