1.3.2      Site Coordination
1.3.2.1            Develop benchmarks for being a &#8220;good&#8221; OSG sites; audit sites against benchmark; and, support sites in reaching conformance

1.3.2.3            Functional/diagnostic testing of OSG sites (identified via operations metrics analysis) resulting in guidance to site owners on how to improve performance.
