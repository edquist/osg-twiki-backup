---+++Operations Center
   * After the initial installation phase, the iVDGL Grid Operations Center will provide coordination support for the OSG ITB. When reporting problems to the iGOC please specify that the problem is related to the OSG ITB.
      * The iGOC email address is <a href="mailto:igoc@ivdgl.org">igoc at ivdgl.org</a>
		* In addition, a <a href="http://igoc.ivdgl.indiana.edu/test/index.php"> web form </a> may be used to open a trouble ticket
	* An archive of communication is <a href="http://listserv.fnal.gov/archives/osg-int.html">given here</a>.
		* All trouble tickets will be reviewed at the weekly <a href="http://igoc.ivdgl.indiana.edu/meeting.php">iVDGL operations meeting</a>, _OSG ITB site administrators are encouraged to attend_
		* A full list of iGOC services and procedures can be found in the [[http://giis.ivdgl.org/twiki/bin/view/Sandbox/WebHome?topic=OpsGuide][OpsGuide]]
