---+++!!Mail List
The current operations model for the OSG ITB is to send mail to the integration mailing list, osg-int@opensciencegrid.org.  An archive of communication is 
<a href="http://listserv.fnal.gov/archives/osg-int.html">given here</a>.
---+++!!Trouble Tickets
A trouble ticketing system targeted for the ITB is being setup by the iGOC.  More information to follow.


---+++Registration with the Operations Center
	* After the initial installation phase, the iVDGL Grid Operations Center will provide coordination support for the OSG ITB. When reporting problems to the iGOC please specify that the problem is related to the OSG ITB.
		* The iGOC email address is <a href="mailto:igoc@ivdgl.org">igoc at ivdgl.org</a>
		* In addition, a <a href="http://igoc.ivdgl.indiana.edu/test/index.php"> web form </a> may be used to open a trouble ticket.
		* The iGOC phone number is +1 317 278 9699
