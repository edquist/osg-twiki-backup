---++ What's getting installed?
%EDITTHIS%

<b>Note:</b> The MIS-CI init.d service is no longer installed in this release of OSG.  There was a cron process associated with that service is no longer needed.  So if you are upgrading from a previous release, you should remove this cron entry for the <i>mis</i> user.
