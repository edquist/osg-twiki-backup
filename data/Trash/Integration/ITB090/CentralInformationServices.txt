---++ OSG Information Management (OIM)
OIM is a collection of administrative and topological information about the OSG.
   * OIM can be found at https://oim.grid.iu.edu
      * A valid IGTF certificate must be available to your browser to access the OIM system

---++ BDII Aggregator
There is a service operated by the GOC which accepts information from each of the compute element's CEMon services, and converts the (GIP) information into an (ldiff) format to be consumed by a BDII service.
   * The address of this service is configured during the compute element install.
   * CEMon runs the GIP every 5 minutes, and pushes this information to the BDII Aggregator
   * The service address of the Aggregator is:
   * There is a GOC operated monitoring service of the BDII Aggregator that alerts the GOC staff when there is a failure.

---++ BDII
A BDII service is operated by the GOC which consumes the BDII Aggregator output.
   * Its current Glue schema version is 1.3

---++ !ReSS
The [[ResourceSelection/WebHome]] service provides a Condor !ClassAd service for CE attributes published with CEMon.  

---++ VirtualOrganizations/VOInfoRS
Virtual Organization Resource Selector provides a web-visible view of published attributes and the results of _grid-scan_ tests, which are essentially [[ValidateSiteVerify][Site Verify]] scripts executed out of the GOC using the =mis= VO. 
   * See http://vors.grid.iu.edu/
