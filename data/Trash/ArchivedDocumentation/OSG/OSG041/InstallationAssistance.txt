---++ Assistance during the installation

If you have questions during the installation of this software, the first line of support is your 
<a href="http://www.grid.iu.edu/osg-includes/sc.php">VO Support Center</a>.

Alternative options  for less formal queries and assistance are:
<OL>
<LI>Knowledge Base<br>
The IU OSG Knowledge Base can be found  <a href="http://kb.grid.iu.edu/">here</a>. Typing OSG as your search string allows you to browse all titles.

<LI>Mailing lists
 <br>The mailing list osg-general@opensciencegrid.org is available for you to submit questions.   
 <br>The <a href="http://listserv.fnal.gov/archives/osg-general.html">osg-general mail list archives</a> are available for viewing.
 <br>More information about the mailing lists is available at 
<a href="http://www.opensciencegrid.org/?pid=1000089">OSG Mailing Lists</a>.

<LI>Community Chat
<br>Real-time help is also available in the
<a href="http://info.opensciencegrid.org/bin/view/Trash/Trash/CommunitySupport/ChatRoom">osg-communitychat room</a> .   

</OL>
