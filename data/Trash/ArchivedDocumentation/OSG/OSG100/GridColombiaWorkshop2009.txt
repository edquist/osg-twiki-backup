---++ Background Information
This will be a two-part workshop focusing on both scientific computing on distributed, Grid-based platforms and Grid infrastructure building.  The workshops will be held at [[http://www.javeriana.edu.co/puj/english/][Universidad Javeriana]], Bogota, Colombia.
   * October 19-22, 2009: Scientific Computing on Grids: _Introductory Grid Computing Overviews and User Capabilities_
   * October 26-29, 2009: Building Grid Infrastructure: _Towards a National Colombian Science Grid_  
  
Goals of the workshop are to:
   * Introduce grid computing concepts from an end-user's perspective
   * Provide hands-on tutorials for end-user client tools
   * Introduce grid infrastructure building principles
   * Provide hands-on tutorials for compute and storage element installation
