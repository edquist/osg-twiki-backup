---++ OSG sites coordination group
The Sites Coordination group meets (via telecon) on a monthly basis, Thursdays, 2:30pm Central.  Announcements will be made to osg-sites@opensciencegrid.org.  There are additionally face to face meetings of site administrators, roughly quarterly.
