---++ Introduction 
The linked document describes the process for installing dCache in an Open Science Grid site using the dCache scripts. For an overview of dCache, see [[http://www.dcache.org/manuals/dcache-whitepaper-light.pdf][&#8220;dCache, the Overview&#8221; (PDF)]]. For further details on installing dCache manually, see Installation in [[http://www.dcache.org/manuals/Book/in.shtml][dCache, the Book]].

Instructions are provided at the VDT site: [[http://vdt.cs.wisc.edu/extras/InstallingDcacheForOSG.html][Installing a dCache System for the Open Science Grid]]
