---++ Testing efforts
The installation, testing, and integration efforts for this release have come from a large number of people from various groups within the OSG community:
   * The OSG Integration testbed core team, and the contributed testbed sites comprising the ITB (including both CE and SE testbeds), see the SiteValidationTable.
   * The VO application developers and testers, also listed in the SiteValidationTable, for providing realistic, end-to-end testing of VO application frameworks, services and user-jobs against the deployed testbed.
   * The OSG UserGroup.WebHome for managing liaison and coordination processes between the OSG user community and the integration team.

---++ Operational support
