---++ Testing efforts
The installation, testing, and integration efforts for this release have come from a large number of people from various groups within the OSG community:
   * The OSG Integration testbed core team, and the contributed testbed sites comprising the ITB (including both CE and SE testbeds), see the SiteValidationTable.
   * The VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo application developers and testers, also listed in the SiteValidationTable, for providing realistic, end-to-end testing of VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo application frameworks, services and user-jobs against the deployed testbed.
   * The OSG VirtualOrganizations for managing liaison and coordination processes between the OSG user community and the integration team.

---++ Operational support
A thanks to the persistent ITB sites, the GOC and Fermilab for providing integration instances for central services required for full grid functional testing and validation.

---++ Documentation efforts
