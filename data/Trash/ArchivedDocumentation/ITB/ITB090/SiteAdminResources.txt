---++ Questions, problems?
   * Send email to osg-sites@opensciencegrid.org for basic installation and configuration questions.
   * Trouble tickets can be opened with the Grid Operations Center by mailing to goc@opensciencegrid.org or visiting this [[http://www.grid.iu.edu/cgi-bin/contact.pl][link]].
   * For storage services support, email osg-storage@opensciencegrid.org.

---++ OSG sites coordination group
The [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/SiteCoordination/WebHome][Sites Coordination]] group meets (via telecon) on a monthly basis, Thursdays, 2:30pm Central.  Announcements will be made to osg-sites@opensciencegrid.org.  There are additionally face to face meetings of site administrators, roughly quarterly.
