---++ CE  Installed Software
%EDITTHIS%

When you install the CE, it comes with quite a bit of software. It includes:

   * Globus, which includes:
      * GRAM 2 (pre-web services)
      * GRAM 4 (web services)
      * !GridFTP
      * RLS
      * RFT
      * GPT
   * Tomcat & Apache
   * !CEMon
   * Make Gridmap
   * Fetch CRL
   * Generic Information Provider (and GLUE Schema)
   * Gratia Probe (depends on your batch system)
   * Java
   * !MonaLisa
   * !MyProxy
   * !MySQL (for RLS and RFT)
   * PRIMA
   * !UberFTP
   * VDS Worker
   * Wget
