---++ Validating BDII

   * Check the GIP validator 
      * http://gip-validate.grid.iu.edu
      * http://is-itb2.grid.iu.edu/cgi-bin/status.cgi
   * Next check your system is reporting via:
<pre>
ldapsearch -x -LLL -p 2170 -h is.grid.iu.edu -b mds-vo-name=YOUR_SITE_NAME_HERE,mds-vo-name=local,o=grid
</pre>
   * If the information if not being reported correctly, run the following on your CE
<pre>
$VDT_LOCATION/lcg/libexec/osg-info-wrapper
</pre>
If this information is correct, then your local GIP is configured correctly.
   * Since BDII gets its information from the GIP through CEMon, also validate the [[ValidateRessAndCemon][ReSS and CEMon]] installation.
