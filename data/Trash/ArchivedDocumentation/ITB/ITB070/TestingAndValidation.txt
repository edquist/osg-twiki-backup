---++ Overview
This is a release description for the OSG ITB 0.7 series of Integration testbed deployments.

   * The CE is based on VDT 1.8.1, see [[http://vdt.cs.wisc.edu/releases/1.8.1/release.html][VDT 1.8.1]]  
   * ITB cache: http://software.grid.iu.edu/itb
