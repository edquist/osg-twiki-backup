---++ Introduction
The accounting service for OSG is provided by the Gratia external project.  Results from Gratia probes run on the compute element are reported to a Gratia collector operated by !FermiGrid/Gratia development team.

---++ Service location and simple usage
The services are located: http://gratia-osg.fnal.gov:8880/gratia-reporting/.  See Trash.ReleaseDocumentationValidateGratia for details about validating a compute element's accounting information.
