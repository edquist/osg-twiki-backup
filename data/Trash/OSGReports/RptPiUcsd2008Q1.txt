Terrence Martin:

OSG activities for the first quarter of 2008 included; WS Gram performance evaluation, including followup performance evaluations to determine the efficacy of updated configurations and code changes. Presentation of the WS Gram performance evaluations at the OSG all hands meeting in March. NFS Lite modifications necessary for NFS Lite to support WS Gram submitted and accepted by the VDT. Support of LIGO VirtualOrganizations/VOInfo user efforts to run  applications at UCSD and at other OSG sites. Testing and presentation of the new osg configuration system at the OSG all hands meeting. Continued site administrator testing and feedback related to the OSG Compute Element development efforts. Deployment and testing of glexec related modifications to support non-shared UID condor environments.

Abhishek Singh Rana:

Abhishek's main focus was on the [[http://datagrid.ucsd.edu/toolkit/][OSG Storage Operations toolkit]]. In addition, he participated in
the OSG user's group coordination, as well as OSG Storage support in general.

maintaining the HLT farm for CMS to OSG in May 2008.
