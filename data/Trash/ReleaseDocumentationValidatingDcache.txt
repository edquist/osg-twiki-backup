*Purpose*: <font color="brown">  The purpose of this document is to demonstrate how to validate a dCache based Storage Element installation.</font>

In order to validate a dCache based SE installation, you need to install the dCache Validation Test Suite provided by the OSG-Storage group.
The complete instructions are available at [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Integration/ITB090/InstallationITBStorageElement#dCache_Validation_Suite][dCache Validation Test Suite]]
