---+++Gratia

The Gratia Project (contributed as an OSG Activity) designs and deploys robust, scalable, trustable and dependable grid accounting and auditing services, publishes an interface to the services and provides a reference implementation. |  [[http://gratia-osg.fnal.gov:8880/gratia-reporting/][<img src="%ATTACHURL%/gratia.png">]]  |
