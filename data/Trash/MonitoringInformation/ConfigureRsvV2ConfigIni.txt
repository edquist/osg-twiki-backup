---+++Configuring !RSV within !OSG wide config.ini (and configure_osg.py)

<p> *IMPORTANT:* This section assumes you've gone through the [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/ReleaseDocumentation/PreparingComputeElement#RSV_Prerequisites][<strong>RSV prerequisites section of the Preparing Compute Element</strong>]] document already.

<p> The recommended and simplest way to configure !RSV on an !OSG CE is by editing the =[RSV]= section in =config.ini=. The section is self-explanatory -- each option is commented upon in detail. You can also refer to the [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/ReleaseDocumentation/ConfigurationFileHelp#Introduction_to_RSV_Section][RSV section of the ConfigureFileHelp]] twiki page for extensive documentation on each of the keys in the [RSV] section of =config.ini=.

<p> *NOTE*: We recommend you *do not enable the !GUMS probe(s)*; it's an experimental probe provided for admins who want to try but it is not guaranteed to work.

<pre class="programlisting">[RSV]
 . . . 
enabled = True
 . . . 
rsv-user = rsvuser
 . . . </pre>

The following snippets from the [RSV] section of =config.ini= (or =extracted-config.ini=) files on production OSG CE's are provided for your benefit: 
   * [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/MonitoringInformation/ConfigIniExampleTier2OsgUchicagoEdu][OSG 1.0.x CE tier2-osg.uchicago.edu - extracted-config.ini]].
   * [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/MonitoringInformation/ConfigIniExampleCitGatekeeper2UltralightOrg][OSG 1.2.x CE cit-gatekeeper2.ultralight.org - extracted-config.ini]].

<p> After editing the config file as shown above, execute:
   * *OSG 1.0 administrators*: <tt> ${VDT_LOCATION}/monitoring/configure-osg.py -c -f &#60;extracted-config.ini or config.ini&#62;</tt>
   * *OSG 1.2 administrators*: <tt> ${VDT_LOCATION}/osg/bin/configure-osg -c -f &#60;extracted-config.ini or config.ini&#62;</tt>

<p> *Alternative Method:* If you would rather do the !RSV configuration on the command line using =configure_osg_rsv=, then refer to the [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/MonitoringInformation/InstallAndConfigureRsvAdvanced][advanced !RSV configuration twiki page]].
