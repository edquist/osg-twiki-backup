---++Configure !RSV within !OSG wide config.ini (using configure-osg)

<p> *IMPORTANT:* Ensure you have gone through the [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/ReleaseDocumentation/PreparingComputeElement#RSV_Prerequisites][<strong>RSV prerequisites section of the Preparing Compute Element</strong>]] document already.

<p> The recommended and simplest way to configure !RSV  (for simple RSV setups)  on an !OSG CE is by using =configure-osg=. 

   * Edit the =[RSV]= section in =config.ini=; The section is self-explanatory -- each option is commented upon in detail. You can also refer to the [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/ReleaseDocumentation/ConfigurationFileHelp#Introduction_to_RSV_Section][RSV section of the ConfigureFileHelp]] twiki page for extensive documentation.
 <pre class="programlisting">[RSV]
enabled = True
rsv-user = rsvuser
 . . . </pre>
      * The following snippets from the [RSV] section of =config.ini= (or =extracted-config.ini=) files on production OSG CE's are provided for your benefit: 
         * [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/MonitoringInformation/ConfigIniExampleTier2OsgUchicagoEdu][OSG 1.0.x CE tier2-osg.uchicago.edu - extracted-config.ini]].
         * [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/MonitoringInformation/ConfigIniExampleCitGatekeeper2UltralightOrg][OSG 1.2.x CE cit-gatekeeper2.ultralight.org - extracted-config.ini]].
      * *NOTE*: We recommend you *do not enable the !GUMS probe(s)*; it's an experimental probe provided for admins who want to try but it is not guaranteed to work.
