---+++ Additional hacks necessary for RSV 3.x to be able to test OSG 1.0.x CEs

<p> To ensure, RSV 3.x is seamlessly able to test an existing OSG 1.0.x CE, please do the following:
   * On your 1.0.x CE, symlink =osg-version= script to new expected location (as of OSG 1.2)
 <pre class="screen"> ln -sf ${VDT_LOCATION}/osg-version ${VDT_LOCATION}/osg/bin/osg-version </pre>
