   * London GLUE schema 1.3 and 2.0 meeting report back


---+ Minutes
   * Participants: Shaowen and others from Iowa , Conrad
   * Shaowen and Gabriele did extensive preparations for the London meeting to present changes to the GLUE schema 1.3. Other paticipants included EGEE and NorduGrid.
   * Purpose of version 1.3 is to augment current 1.2 implementation, while keeping the same design.
   * Proposed changes were received well and OSG and EGEE will move forward with implementation.
   * A web page tracking changes has been set up at (Update when link is available)

   * On the second day of the conference the plans for schema version 2.0 were discussed. 
   * Purpose of 2.0 is a major redesign. OSG and EGEE goal is to ensure interoperability to support LHC data analysis.
   * Open question whether to to include version 2.0 in OGF. Next OGF meeting in Raleigh, NC in January 2007 (?).
   * Need to discuss with OSG management whether a push to include 2.0 in OGF should be made.
   * No consensus could be reached at the meeting on timelines for development of version 2.0
