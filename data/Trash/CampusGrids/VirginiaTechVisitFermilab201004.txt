---++ Introduction
   * Date: 2010/04/19/10 to 2010/04/20
   * Location: Fermilab
   * Description: Visit by Virginia Tech staff to Fermilab to see and hear about implementing campus grids, managing OSG installation, and the like.

---++ Follow up Action Items

   * VTech will look at what machine/VM to do a first install of an OSG CE on and for a means to deploy a new machine for this service. 
   * Roger will work with Leo to ensure there are usable clients available VTech to run first Belle or DayaBay applications on a remote OSG site.
   * Ruth will follow up with Jim Basney and the CILOGON project for status of integration of Shib tokens with generation of usable X509 certificates  to use on OSG. 
   * Steve Timm will be the OSG contact for the short term.
   * Feedback from VTech team on the visit: Include an overview of OSG And terminology early in the agenda; include a few simple diagrams as part of this.
