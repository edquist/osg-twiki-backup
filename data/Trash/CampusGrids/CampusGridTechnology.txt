---+ Submitting Multiple Jobs with Condor

---++ About this Document
This document describes the technology being developed for a campus grid.

---++ Prototype
The campus grid prototype will use Condor to [[http://www.cs.wisc.edu/condor/manual/v7.4/5_2Connecting_Condor.html][Flock]] between on campus resources.  Authentication and Authorization will be handled through non-cert Condor security mechanisms.  Flocking between two Condor resources is simple.  Flocking between non-Condor resource will require a glidein mechanism.  The glidein mechanism should be as simple as possible.
