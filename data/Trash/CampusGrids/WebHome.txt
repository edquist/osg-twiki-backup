Outreach

This activity supports the following endeavors:
Use of the OSG software stack and Virtual Data Toolkit (VDT) for users and projects outside of OSG

&#8226; APAC National Grid  http://grid.apac.edu.au/
&#8226; EGEE (Enabling Grids for E-science)
&#8226; LCG (Worldwide LHC Computing Grid)
&#8226; LSC Data Grid
&#8226; OxGrid, Oxford e-Research Centre
Collaborative Projects with Partners

&#8226; Campus Infrastructure Days between the OSG, TeraGrid, Internet2 and Educause .
&#8226; Grid Interoperability Now (GIN), especially peer-to-peer application interoperation with Pacific Rim Applications and Grid Middleware Assembly (PRAGMA).
&#8226; Researchers from the University of Buenos Aires are reusing the OSG Grid School material for their local training activities.
Support for individuals learning to use or evaluating the use of the OSG

The OSG Virtual Organization (link to be added) is supported by OSG staff. Individuals may apply for membership in this VO as a gateway to using the OSG. To&#8211;date, about 20 individuals make use of this facility.
Help for campus infrastructures self-organizing into a local shared infrastructure. 
Contact us at eot@opensciencegrid.org.
