---+!! Bosco with MPI

This document will describe how to submit MPI jobs with Bosco.

---++ Syntax
<pre class="file">
+REMOTE_SMPGranularity = 16
+REMOTE_NodeNumber = 16
</pre>

Will result in a line in PBS like:
<pre class="screen">
#PBS -l nodes=1:ppn=16
</pre>
