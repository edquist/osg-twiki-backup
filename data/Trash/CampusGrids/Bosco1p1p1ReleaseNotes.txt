---++ Changes

Bosco Version 1.1.1 fixes 2 issues.
   * [[https://htcondor-wiki.cs.wisc.edu/index.cgi/tktview?tn=3429][Condor #3429]] - Fixes a failure when submitting to Condor clusters that have long history of jobs.
   * [[https://htcondor-wiki.cs.wisc.edu/index.cgi/tktview?tn=3430][Condor #3430]] - Fixes an error case when submitting to a SGE cluster that the blahp would error, but not exit with non-zero exit status.

---++ Upgrading from Bosco 1.1

Upgrading instructions can be found on the bosco [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Trash/Trash/CampusGrids/BoscoInstall#4_6_How_to_Update_BOSCO][installation documents]].  The upgrade should be trivial from 1.1, just replacing the Bosco install, and re-adding the clusters.
