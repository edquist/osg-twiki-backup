---++Introduction
       The GUMS installation tutorial will proceed in two parts.  In this part of the hands on exercise we will show
how to configure a single GUMS server without high availabilty. In the next part we will show how FermiGrid configures
a High Availability GUMS server 
---++Requirements
       For the first part of the exercise only one machine, real or virtual, is required.  If you can acquire a service 
certificate in advance (http/yourhostname.yourdomain), please do so, otherwise grid-admins will be available at 
the meeting to help.
---++More Information
      A document is available in Google Docs which will be the full text of the FermiGrid HA tutorial.
     For purposes of this tutorial, $VDT_LOCATION is /usr/local/vdt-2.0.0.
