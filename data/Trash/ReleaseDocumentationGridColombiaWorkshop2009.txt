---++ Workshop Information
   * This will be a two-part workshop focusing on both scientific computing on distributed, Grid-based platforms and Grid infrastructure building
      * October 19-23, 2009, [[http://www.javeriana.edu.co/puj/english/][Universidad Javeriana]], Bogota, Colombia: Introductory Grid Computing Overviews and User Capabilities
      * October 26-29, 2009, [[http://www.uniandes.edu.co/][Universidad de los Andes]], Bogota, Colombia: Building Grid Infrastructure, Towards a National Colombian Grid  
   * Goals of the workshop are to:
      * Introduce grid computing concepts from an end-user's perspective
      * Introduce natural grid infrastructure building principles including building and operating a grid operations center
      * Provide hands-on tutorials for end-user client tools
      * Provide hands-on tutorials for compute and storage element installations
