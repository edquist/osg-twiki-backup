---++ Workshop Information
   * Date/location: October 19-23, 2009, Bogota, Columbia
   * Goals of the workshop are to:
      * Introduce grid computing concepts from an end-user's perspective
      * Introduce natural grid infrastructure building principles including building and operating a grid operations center
      * Provide hands-on tutorials for end-user client tools
      * Provide hands-on tutorials for compute and storage element installations
