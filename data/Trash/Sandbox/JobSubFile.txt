charmmJob.py (Job-Sub-File)

Reads in parameters exported by charmmJob.sh as PANDA_JOB_PARAMS. Reads in command-line parameters as site ('TestPilot' by default), transformation (Setup-Script to be executed at remote resource), jobid ('0' by default), and name ('CHARMM Job' by default). Creates an instance of 'JobSpec' class, fills in values for all the necessary variables and submits the job.
