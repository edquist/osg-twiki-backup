Before you install you'll need a few things:
    1 DOE grid certificate 
    2 DOE host certificate 
Getting these will require some time so it's best to do these well in advance of installing your CE.

----+++ Getting a DOE grid certificate
A DOE grid certificate is a certificate for individuals that will allow you to identify yourself when access grid services.  It is needed to submit jobs to grid sites and for obtaining a host certificate for your new system.
