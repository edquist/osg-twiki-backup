    <ul>
      <li>Respond to request from Igor M. for statements on !MyISAM:
   1. Tables are locked for writes *and* reads during backup procedures.
   1. Write operations *and* read queries are, "usually" locked during long queries. Exact criteria for locking are not known but are empirically quite esoteric.
   1. Foreign key constraints are not honored.
   1. Query times blow up as data scanned increases (see incomplete draft TWiki page).
