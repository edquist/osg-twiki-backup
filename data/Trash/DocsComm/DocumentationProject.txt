---++ About Creating Twiki Pages

The is the twiki space to be used to organize various OSG web development activities. It is a resource available to, and editable by, all OSG users. If you're new, register at [[http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/bin/view/TWiki/TWikiRegistration][TWikiRegistration]]; Leigh will then have to add you to the [[http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/bin/view/Main/GridGroup][GridGroup]] to allow you write access. 

To edit content, click on the 'Edit' button at either the top or bottom of any page and you will see how the pages are constructed. Simply add new text and click 'Preview' then (if it looks okay) click on 'Save'. Creating new pages happens automatically whenever you write a new (i.e. currently unused) [[http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/bin/view/TWiki/WikiWord][WikiWord]]. This is denoted by a ? after the word - clicking on the ? creates the new [[http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/bin/view/TWiki/WikiWord][WikiWord]] (also known as a 'topic'). Please feel free to correct any mistakes, and to add new information (using _[[http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/bin/view/TWiki/GoodStyle][GoodStyle]]_ of course)!
