---++ Procedure to Post Research Highlights


1. Create new web page for RH
 
   a. In cms, create a new HTML page that is *online, but not visible*.  The title should be the same as the title of the RH.
   a. Copy contents of a previous RH to make it easier so you can just cut and paste. Be sure to update titles, etc.
   a. Images within the tables should be cropped and resized to 200 x 200 pixels
   a. For "click for larger image" just use the original files. 
   a. This might be a good time to work with the other versions of images (see steps 4 and 6 below). Be sure to name them logically; the file name shows in the browser.

2. In cms, relocate web page so it is under the Science > > Research Highlights Section (make sure Visible is checked in the left menu)

3. Change properties of page (click *visible* checkbox in cms)

4. Give the page a "Friendly URL name".

5. Update [[http://www.opensciencegrid.org/?pid=1000074][Research Highlights]] page
   a. Image for this page should be cropped square to 90x90 pixels and put in  [[%ATTACHURL%/orange_box.psd][Orange box]] (Photoshop format) - *save the image at 90x90 without the orange box for step #5.*

6. Update [[http://www.opensciencegrid.org/?pid=1000060][News and Events]] page, "News from OSG" section.  Use 90x90 pixel image, without the orange box.

7. Update [[http://www.opensciencegrid.org][OSG home page]]
   a. Crop a hi-res image so width=300, height=105; you will have to play with resizing to get a compelling graphic.
   a. Name this image *homepage_mainimage.jpg*
   a. Follow the existing design of page, adding the appropriate caption, title and pulling the first paragraph or so of text from the article.
   a. upload homepage_mainimage.jpg using the cms image manager (it will overwrite previous without asking) or FTP .


Details and misc. notes:  

   * (jn 7feb07)Could do step 1 and work with images a couple days ahead of time. Also the short text for home page, news & events and science on the osg.  
   * mt:  if using a large image, use the FTP site to upload
