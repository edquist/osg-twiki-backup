---++Objectives and Scope
This OSG Information Services Activity evaluates existing information services deployed within the OSG and emerging information services. This activity conducts gap analysis and helps OSG make informed decisions about choosing and integrating appropriate information services. 

OSG collaborates on the Glue Schema activity with the EGEE, Globus and the broader community. 

The ongoing work is discussed as part of the  [[MonitoringInformation][Monitoring and Information Activity (MIG)]] weekly meetings on a Monday.

---++Information Services Activity Documents
The InformationServicesDocuments provides pointers to additional documents and references.
