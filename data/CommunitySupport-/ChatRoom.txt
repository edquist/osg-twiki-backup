   * [[http://exodus.jabberstudio.org/][Exodus]] (Windows)
   * [[http://www.trillian.cc/][Trillian Pro]] (Windows, non-free)

Download one and install it.
Here are [[ChatRoomClientDocs][client-specific documentation and examples]].


---+++ Register your Jabber ID 

Go to the [[http://dsd.lbl.gov/cgi-bin/jabber/registration/register.cgi][registration page]] at LBL.
Fill out the appropriate fields: we suggest putting "OSG" for project and your VO name for org.
You will not be able to sign in to the server unless you perform this step!

---+++ Sign on to the jabber service

Configuration is dependent on your client.  The server name is *jabber.dsd.lbl.gov*, the port is *5223*, and the username and password are the ones you chose during the registration step.  If you can't connect, try disabling SSL (the non-SSL port is 5222).

---+++ Log in to the osg-community chatroom

This may also be client dependent.  The room name is *osg-community*, and the server is *conference.jabber.dsd.lbl.gov*.  Gaim
lets you specify this information separately; some clients (such as ayttm) may need it input as *osg-community@conference.jabber.dsd.lbl.gov*.
