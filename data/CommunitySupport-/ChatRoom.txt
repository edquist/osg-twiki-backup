---+++ Clients

Clients that support jabber (in no particular order)

	* [[http://gaim.sourceforge.net/][GAIM]] (Linux/Windows)
	* [[http://psi.affinix.com/][Psi]] (Linux/Windows/Macintosh)
	* [[http://ayttm.sourceforge.net/][ayttm]] (Linux)
	* [[http://www.apple.com/macosx/features/ichat/][iChat]] (Macintosh OS 10.4+)
	* [[http://www.adiumx.com][Adium]] (Macintosh)
	* [[http://www.miranda-im.org][Miranda]] (Windows)
	* [[http://exodus.jabberstudio.org/][Exodus]] (Windows)
	* [[http://www.trillian.cc/][Trillian Pro]] (Windows, non-free)

Download one and install it.

---+++ Register your Jabber ID 

Go to the [[http://dsd.lbl.gov/cgi-bin/jabber/registration/register.cgi][registration page]] at LBL.
Fill out the appropriate fields: we suggest putting "OSG" for project and your VO name for org.
You will not be able to sign in to the server unless you perform this step!

---+++ Sign on to the jabber service

Configuration is dependent on your client.  The server name is jabber.dsd.lbl.gov, the port is 5223, and the username and password are the ones you chose during the registration step.  An example configuration for gaim is shown at the bottom of the page.

---+++ Log in to the osg-community chatroom

This may also be client dependent.  The room name is osg-community, and the server is conference.dsd.lbl.gov.  Gaim
lets you specify this information separately; some clients (such as ayttm) may need it input as osg-community@conference.dsd.lbl.gov.
