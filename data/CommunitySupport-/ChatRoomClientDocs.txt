#AnchorExodus
---+++ Exodus
Here is an example how to configure an Exodus client:<br>
Start Exodus and click on "Clisk to Sign On".
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/pub/CommunitySupport/ChatRoomClientDocs/Exodus1.PNG
<br>
A "Login" window will open with a "Profile" link at the left side. Click on that too
and then select "Create New Profile...".
<br>
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/pub/CommunitySupport/ChatRoomClientDocs/Exodus2.PNG
<br>
You'll get a small window "New Profile" that lets you name your profile - I called one mine OSG.
<br>
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/pub/CommunitySupport/ChatRoomClientDocs/Exodus2a.PNG
<br>
After you name your profile and click "OK" you get yet another window
(hey, it's Windows after all....) so that you can finally set details. Only the first two tabs need
to be set (two following pictures).
<br>
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/pub/CommunitySupport/ChatRoomClientDocs/Exodus3.PNG
<br>
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/pub/CommunitySupport/ChatRoomClientDocs/Exodus4.PNG
<br>
Fill it out as shown above. click "OK" and go back to the "Login" window. Select 
the newlly defined profile and connect. You'll be connected to the server, we still 
need to find the chat room. 
<p>
At this point you've got only one Exodus window, Select "Exodus" from a horizontal menu at the top
and click on "Join a Chatroom":<br>
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/pub/CommunitySupport/ChatRoomClientDocs/Exodus5.PNG<br>
.... and sure enough another window will open.....<br>
Fill it out, click on "Finish" and you'll meet us all...
