#AnchorIchat
---+++ iChat

Here is an example Jabber configuration for iChat. After you registered at [[http://dsd.lbl.gov/cgi-bin/jabber/registration/register.cgi][the registration page]] and defined your username, you go to iChat and open the Preference Pane in the Menu *iChat->Preferences*. You change to the *Accounts* tab

    <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/iChat_1.jpg" alt="iChat_1.jpg"  width="517" height="467"  />

and add a new Jabber account using the plus sign at the lower left corner.

The *Account Type* has to be *Jabber*, the *Jabber ID* your username with the server name *jabber.dsd.lbl.gov* added after the username with an *@*. As a description, you can use *OSG* for example.

    <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/iChat_2.jpg" alt="iChat_2.jpg"  width="351" height="225"  />

After you added your account, change to the *Server Settings* tab of your crated Jabber account of the *Accounts* tab of the *iChat Preferences Pane*. Here, you switch off *Connect using SSL* when your Mac OS X firewall is activated and if you have problems connecting to the Jabber server. In the *Account Information* tab, you make sure to select *Use this account* and decide whether you like iChat to automatically log in to your Jabber account at startup.

    <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/iChat_4.jpg" alt="iChat_4.jpg"  width="517" height="467"  />

To access the community chat room, Use *Go to Chat...* in the *File* menu

   * iChat_5.jpg: <br />
    <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/iChat_5.jpg" alt="iChat_5.jpg"  width="254" height="215"  />

and enter the full name of the osg-community chat room *osg-community@conference.dsd.lbl.gov*

    <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/iChat_6.jpg" alt="iChat_6.jpg"  width="360" height="150"  />

The group chat window opens

    <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/iChat_7.jpg" alt="iChat_7.jpg"  width="879" height="365"  />

showing all online participants. To distinguish the participants, activate *Show Names* or *Show Names and Pictures* in the *View* menu.

#AnchorExodus
---+++ Exodus
Here is an example how to configure an Exodus client:<br>
Start Exodus and click on "Clisk to Sign On".
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/pub/CommunitySupport/ChatRoomClientDocs/Exodus1.PNG
<br>
A "Login" window will open with a "Profile" link at the left side. Click on that too
and then select "Create New Profile...".
<br>
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/pub/CommunitySupport/ChatRoomClientDocs/Exodus2.PNG
<br>
You'll get a small window "New Profile" that lets you name your profile - I called one mine OSG.
<br>
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/pub/CommunitySupport/ChatRoomClientDocs/Exodus2a.PNG
<br>
After you name your profile and click "OK" you get yet another window
(hey, it's Windows after all....) so that you can finally set details. Only the first two tabs need
to be set (two following pictures).
<br>
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/pub/CommunitySupport/ChatRoomClientDocs/Exodus3.PNG
<br>
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/pub/CommunitySupport/ChatRoomClientDocs/Exodus4.PNG
<br>
Fill it out as shown above. click "OK" and go back to the "Login" window. Select 
the newlly defined profile and connect. You'll be connected to the server, we still 
need to find the chat room. 
<p>
At this point you've got only one Exodus window, Select "Exodus" from a horizontal menu at the top
and click on "Join a Chatroom":<br>
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/pub/CommunitySupport/ChatRoomClientDocs/Exodus5.PNG<br>
.... and sure enough another window will open.....<br>
Fill it out, click on "Finish" and you'll meet us all...
http://osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/pub/CommunitySupport/ChatRoomClientDocs/Exodus6.PNG<br>
