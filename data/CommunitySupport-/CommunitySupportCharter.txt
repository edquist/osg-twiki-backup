---++ Draft Community Support Center (CSC) Charter

This activity will facilitate the provision of Support on the Open Science Grid
for those entities that do not have a Support Center and are thus not
explicitly included in the 
<a href="http://osg-docdb.opensciencegrid.org/cgi-bin/ShowDocument?docid=47">OSG 
Operations Model</a>.	There are three components to the activity:

---+++ Design and Infrastructure

	* Management: leadership and process
	* Support mechanisms
		* Community discussion (lists/bulletin boards/chat rooms)
		* Dedicated issue tracking (roundup/bugzilla/etc)
	* Knowledgebase generation from support mechanisms
	* Scope: will be completed when Deployment commences

---+++ Deployment

	* Documentation for new OSG users
	* Marketing and Advertising
	* Scope: will be completed when Operations commences

---+++ Operations

	* Support mechanism maintenance
	* Scope: Ongoing
