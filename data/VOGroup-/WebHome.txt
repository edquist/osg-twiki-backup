---++ Overview

The User Support group aims to provide help and information to VOs and application owners; and to represent the users' requirements and interests to OSG.

---++ Meetings

[[UserSupportMeetings][Meetings]] take place every week on Thursday at %RED%<strong>noon Chicago time (NOTE CHANGE)</strong>%ENDCOLOR%. Every second week will have a more open agenda to enable users to bring up issues of interest to them. Please see our [[UserSupportMeetings][meetings]] page for coordinates and agendas of past and future meetings.

---++ Current Activities

   * Work with VOs on current problems, especially resource utilization as needed for specific runs.
   * [[OSGUserSurveys][OSG User satisfaction surveys]] for Quarter 2 '07 (03/15 - 06/15). 
   * [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Integration/ITB_0_7/SiteValidationTable#VO_validation][VO application validation of ITB 0.7.x]] towards release of OSG 0.8.0. In partnership with OSG Integration Group.
   * An extensible [[OsgVoSiteTests][VO-centric site testing package]].
