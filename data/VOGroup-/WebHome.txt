---++ Strategic Goals

<img alt="" src="%PUBURL%/Sandbox/AbhishekSinghRanaSandbox/AcceleratedDrive_UG.png" />

<font color="#0066ff"> *1.* </font><font color="#003399"> *Provide Science input to the OSG Facility, and to the complete OSG software engineering lifecycle.* </font> <br /><font color="#0066ff"> *2.* </font><font color="#003399"> *Promote decisive drivers, firmly based on Science needs, for different areas of grid technology in OSG.* </font> <br /><font color="#0066ff"> *3.* </font><font color="#003399"> *Using impulse from Science needs, help evolve core technologies, explore emerging technologies.* </font> <br /><font color="#0066ff"> *4.* </font><font color="#003399"> *Serve as catalyst, if needed, for transition of new technologies into effective use by stakeholders.* </font> <br /><font color="#0066ff"> *5.* </font><font color="#003399"> *Provide accelerated support to major Science stakeholders - to help increase each's utilization of OSG.* </font> <br /><font color="#0066ff"> *6.* </font><font color="#003399"> *Provide extended level of support to avenues for engaging new, and diversified, Science communities.* </font> <br /><font color="#0066ff"> *7.* </font><font color="#003399"> *Facilitate a platform for transition of new Science communities to develop into established users.* </font> <br /><font color="#0066ff"> *8.* </font><font color="#003399"> *Serve as an ad-hoc platform for communication across [[http://www.opensciencegrid.org/VO_List][all VOs]] in OSG.* </font>

---++ Activities & Focus Areas

%ICON{more}% <font color="#0066ff"> *VO Planning and Science Requirements towards Future Releases of OSG* </font>
   * [[VOApplicationsRequirements][OSG 1.0/1.2 Planning: Requirements]] set forth by stakeholders. 
   * Facilitating VOs' Science requirements to serve as direct input to OSG software engineering plans. 
   * In partnership with OSG Project Management and Middleware/VDT team. 

%ICON{more}% <font color="#0066ff"> *VO Validation of Sites on Integration Testbed* </font>
   * VO application validation of ITB to enable official releases of OSG, in close-knit partnership with OSG Integration and OSG Storage. 
   * Highly structured activity: Each participating stakeholder exercises a stake in vetting, suggesting changes, and approving readiness of an official OSG release. 
   * Statistics from ITB 0.9.x %ICON{external}% OSG 1.0 validation cycle: 
      * 12 participating Science stakeholders/VOs, 7 VOs ran actual Science applications. 
      * 6 VOs participated in Storage validation, of which, 4 VOs in Opportunistic Storage validation. 
      * Coalition of 36+ experts, 20+ from VO communities. 
      * After rigorous validation and active feedback, official 'green flags' toward OSG 1.0 were given by ATLAS, CDF, CIGI, CMS, DOSAR, Dzero, Engage VO, Fermilab VO, LIGO, nanoHUB, SBGrid, and SDSS/DES. 
   * Archive: [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/ReleaseDocumentation/SiteValidationTable#VO_validation][ITB 0.9.x %ICON{external}% OSG 1.0]] , [[UserGroup.SiteValidationTable_ITB_07_Archived#VO_validation][ITB 0.7.x %ICON{external}% OSG 0.8]] , [[UserGroup.SiteValidationTable_ITB_05_Archived][ITB 0.5.x %ICON{external}% OSG 0.6]] . 

%ICON{more}% <font color="#0066ff"> *Development of a Site Testing Package for use by VOs* </font>
   * Development of a [[OsgVoSiteTests][Extensible VO-centric Site Testing Package]] - to be of high-utility for all VOs. 
   * Site Testing Package is ready for adoption and usage by VOs, alongside OSG 1.0. 
   * [[OsgVoSiteTests#Overview][Overview]] | [[OsgVoSiteTests#Download_Source][Download]] | [[OsgVoSiteTests#Getting_Started][Use]] | [[mailto:osg-user-group?subject=osg-vo-tests][Give feedback]] 

%ICON{more}% <font color="#0066ff"> *OSG VO* </font>
   * Coordination of OSG VO. 
   * Platform for transition of new Science communities to develop into established OSG Users and VOs. 
   * In partnership with, and bootstrapping from, OSG Engagement and OSG Education Outreach Training (EOT). 

%ICON{more}% <font color="#0066ff"> *Enabling MPI Resource Advertisement, Discovery, and Usage* </font>
   * Work is in progress with OSG Engagement and !ReSS teams, MPI Resource providers, OSG Operations (GOC). 

%ICON{more}% <font color="#0066ff"> *Security Policies and Procedures related to VOs* </font>
   * Partnership with OSG Security Office to enable matters related to VOs. 

%ICON{more}% <font color="#0066ff"> *Accelerated Drive to enable Science* </font>
   * Targeted work to accelerate OSG's support for specific VOs who are producing Science results. | [[AcceleratedDrive][Work in progress]] 
   * Focus is on value-addition in Science, besides LHC, and spans across broad areas within OSG technology lifecycle. 
   * Ensuring technology's readiness, availability, site adoption, deployment, user adoption, operations, troubleshooting. 
   * In partnership with OSG Project Management, OSG Sites including Validation/Integration/Production, OSG Troubleshooting, OSG Operations (GOC), Middleware/VDT including external technical projects. 

%ICON{more}% <font color="#0066ff"> *Evolving Workload Management capabilities of OSG* </font>
   * Moving towards more user-friendly and efficient access of VOs to computational resources across multiple sites. 
   * Ensuring adequate security and interoperability in workload management with peer Grids. 

%ICON{more}% <font color="#0066ff"> *Evolving and Strengthening the Data-Grid capabilities of OSG* </font>
   * Partnership with OSG Storage to gradually enable opportunistic usage of deployed storage. 

%ICON{more}% <font color="#0066ff"> *OSG Biannual VO Usage Feedback and Direct Dialog* </font>
   * Forthcoming, Direct Dialog for Quarter 2 '08. | [[Draft][Draft in preparation]] 
   * OSG User satisfaction surveys, introduced in Quarter 3 '07. | Archive: [[OSGUserSurveys][Quarter 3 '07]] 

%ICON{more}% <font color="#0066ff"> *Miscellaneous* </font>
   * Work with VOs on current problems, especially resource utilization as needed for specific runs. 
   * VOs' management of Job submission and execution, Data movement and storage, Security and Policy.

---++ Mailing Lists & News

The <u>OSG Users list</u> ( =osg-users at opensciencegrid dot org=), [[http://listserv.fnal.gov/archives/osg-users.html][archived here]], consists of representatives of all officially registered stakeholding VOs in the OSG, and is the primary medium for circulation of important announcements and news items. This list also serves as a general VO/user discussion forum. Representatives of newly formed VOs are strongly encouraged to [[http://listserv.fnal.gov/scripts/wa.exe?SUBED1=osg-users&A=1][subscribe to the list]]. A very small subset of announcements is occasionally also posted on the [[UserGroupNewsItems][news page]].

The <u>OSG User Group Coordination list</u> ( =osg-user-group at opensciencegrid dot org=) consists of the OSG User Group Coordinators, OSG Applications Coordinators, and the OSG Executive Director. A VO interested in communicating with the coordination team can feel free to [[mailto:OSG-USER-GROUP@OPENSCIENCEGRID.ORG][send an email to this list]] anytime.
