---+++ *Date and time*

Every Thursday at %RED%<strong>noon Chicago time (NOTE CHANGE)</strong>%ENDCOLOR%.

---+++ *How to attend*

---++++!! If you are near a computer ...
   1. Go to the [[https://fnal.webex.com/fnal/j.php?ED=95855797&UID=0][WebEx coordinates]] for the meeting.
   1. Enter your name, your email address, and the meeting password (=UserSupport=).
   1. Enter your 'phone number to receive a callback; or, if you are brave, use the WebEx client to initiate VOIP.

---++++!! If you are not near a computer ...
   1. Call in toll (650-429-3300) or toll-free (866-469-3239).
   1. When prompted, enter the meeting number (567 491 540).

Please note: the callback method costs the OSG less than the toll free number; so please use the callback method if possible.
