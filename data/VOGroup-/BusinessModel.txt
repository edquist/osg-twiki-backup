---++ Strategic Goals

   * Engage Science Communities.
   * Foster new Communities to develop into established OSG Users and VOs.
   * Catalyze Science to drive OSG Middleware, Deployment, and Operations.
   * Enable Science on Production-scale OSG Facility.
   * Accelerate and Strengthen OSG Procedures and Processes.
