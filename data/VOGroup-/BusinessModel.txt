---++ Strategic Goals

<font size=+0>
   * To engage, and be engaged with, Science Communities - <i> OSG stakeholders</i>.
   * To foster new Communities to develop into established OSG Users and VOs.
   * To catalyze Science to drive OSG Middleware, Deployment, and Operations.
   * To enable Science on Production-scale OSG Facility.
   * To accelerate OSG's support for Communities producing Science results on OSG.
   * To accelerate and further strengthen OSG Procedures and Processes.
</font>
