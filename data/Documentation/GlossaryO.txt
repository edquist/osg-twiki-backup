---+ Glossary O

This topic holds the terms beginning with "O" and will be included in the GlossaryOfTerms topic.

%STARTINCLUDE%
%INCLUDE{ "GlossaryJumpIndex" }%
%EDITTHIS%

   $ OGSA<a name="DefsOgsa"></a>: _See_ [[#DefsOpenGridServicesArchitecture][Open Grid Services Architecture]].

   $ OIM<a name="DefsOim"></a>: _See_ [[#DefsOpenScienceGridInformationManagement][Open Science Grid Information Management]]

   $ Open Grid Services Architecture<a name="DefsOpenGridServicesArchitecture"></a>: represents an evolution towards a Grid system architecture based on Web services concepts and technologies. _See also_ Globus's [[http://www.globus.org/ogsa/][Open Grid Services Architecture (OGSA) site]].

   $ Open Science Grid Information Management<a name="DefsOpenScienceGridInformationManagement"></a>: A service that defines the topology used by various OSG systems and services; it is based on the OSG Blueprint Document available at http://osg-docdb.opensciencegrid.org/cgi-bin/ShowDocument?docid=18. For example, !MyOSG, BDII, Gratia all use topology defined in OIM.

   $ Ownership: A state of having absolute or well-defined partial rights and responsibilities for a Resource depending on the type of control. OSG considers two such types: actual Ownership and Ownership by virtue of a Contract/Lease. A Lessee is a limited Owner of the Resource for the duration of the Contract/Lease.
