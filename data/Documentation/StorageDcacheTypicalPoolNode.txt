---++ Storage In a Box systems
These are cost effective installations and come in two varieties, classic and initiation..

---+++ Classic 
   * 3Ware, Areca PCI-based multi-channel Raid N controller
   * standard 2-CPU board
   * 4 GB memory
   * Gigabit ethernet\
   * 12-16 SATA disks (250-500 GB)

<p style="text-align: center;"><img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/storage_in_box_obvious.gif" alt="Storage In A Box (obvious)" style="width:95%" /><br>Storage In A Box (obvious)</p>


---+++ Initiation
   * X4500 from Sun
   * 24 TB
   * ZFS (Solaris)
   * 4 x GigE
