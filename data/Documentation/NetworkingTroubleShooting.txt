This page will eventually include pointers to various network troubleshooting resources.   In general, when users suspect  a network problem the procedure is:

   * Describe the problem
   * Gather relevant data
   * Contact the next line of support if the problem persists

We have a draft  version of the OSG [[%ATTACHURL%/20130204-OSG-Debug-SPM.docx][network debugging document]] that describes things in much more detail.  If you have comments, questions or suggestions, please contact Shawn McKee.
