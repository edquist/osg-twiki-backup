---++List of Responsibilities
Each document in the Documentation Web in the OSG TWiki has an owner.
The owner of a document should have knowledge about the topic of the document and is responsible for:
   1. Creating the document (if it doesn't exist)
   1. Updating the document when:
      * Errors are discovered (by document users or the Integration Team)
      * Software or other changes make the document out of date
      * New OSG releases require a change in the document
      * The document is identified by the Ongoing Evaluation/Improvement Process as needing improvement (up to 2 times a year)
   1. Working in concert with the Reviewer of the document to resolve issues identified by the Reviewer after each significant update
   1. Notifying the Document Architect if unable to continue to fulfill the responsibilities of the Owner
