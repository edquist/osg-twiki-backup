---+++ Repo Requirements

The OSG RPMs currently support Red Hat Enterprise Linux 5 and variants (Scientific Linux 5 and !CentOS 5). Currently most of our testing has been done on Scientific Linux 5: your mileage may vary.

   1. Install EPEL: <pre class="rootscreen">
%UCL_PROMPT_ROOT% wget http://download.fedoraproject.org/pub/epel/5/i386/epel-release-5-4.noarch.rpm
%UCL_PROMPT_ROOT% rpm -i epel-release-5-4.noarch.rpm </pre>
   1. Install the osg repositories: <pre class="rootscreen">
%UCL_PROMPT_ROOT% rpm -Uvh http://vdt.cs.wisc.edu/repos/3.0/el5/development/x86_64/osg-release-3.0-3.noarch.rpm </pre>
