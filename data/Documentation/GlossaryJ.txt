   $ Job: An executable set of code submitted to and run on the [[GlossaryOfTerms#DefsResource][Resources]] provided by the [[GlossaryOfTerms#DefsGrid][Grid]]. A job is the fundamental unit of work in a Grid infrastructure.

#DefsJobManager
   $ Job Manager: A Globus term that refers to a program used to interface to the batch system on the remote side. The spelling is case sensitive when used with Globus client tools. Supported job managers are =fork=, =managedfork=, =ccs=, =condor=, =lsf=, =pbs= and =sge=. The =fork= job manager is the default job manager. It executes the job directly on the gatekeeper.
