%EDITTHIS%

   $ Job: An executable set of code; in OSG, an executable to be submitted to run on OSG resources.

#RefsJobManager
   $ Job manager: A Globus term that refers to a program used to refer to the batch system on the remote side. The spelling is case sensitive when used with Globus client tools. Supported job managers are =fork=, =managedfork=, =ccs=, =condor=, =lsf=, =pbs= and =sge=. The =fork= job manager is the default job manager. It executes the job directly on the gatekeeper.
