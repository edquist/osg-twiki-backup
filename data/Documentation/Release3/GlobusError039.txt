---++Globus Error: 39

---+++Syntax:

=GRAM Job submission failed because the provided RSL string includes variables that could not be identified (error code 39)=

---+++Known causes:

This error occurs when Globus fails to recognize one of the macros included in the RSL.

---+++Recommendations:

Check the spelling of macros and that the path it refers to exists
