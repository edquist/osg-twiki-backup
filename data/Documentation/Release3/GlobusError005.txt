---++Globus Error: 5

---+++Syntax:

=GRAM Job failed because the executable does not exist (error code 5)=

---+++Known causes:

This occurs when the client attempts to run an executable on the remote site and the remote site cannot locate that executable.

---+++Recommendations:

Check the spelling on the executable's name and verify that your program attempts to invoke the executable at the proper location on the remote site as well as the fact that you have permission to execute in that location.
