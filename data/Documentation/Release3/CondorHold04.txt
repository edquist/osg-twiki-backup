---++Condor Hold Code 4

---+++Known causes:

Hold Code 4 indicates that the proxy associated with the job was corrupted, expired or otherwise invalid.
Note that OSG requires a valid VOMS proxy, not just a Grid-proxy.  Use <verbatim>voms-proxy-init</verbatim>

---+++Recommendations:

Renew your proxy and release the job.
