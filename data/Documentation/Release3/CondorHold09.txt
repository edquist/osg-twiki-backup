---++Condor Hold Code 9

---+++Syntax

=Code 9 subcode &lt;unix_errno&gt;=

---+++Known causes:

Hold Code 9 indicates that condor could not open the standard output stream.

---+++Recommendations:

Check your firewall settings and see [[CondorFirewalls][Condor ports]].
