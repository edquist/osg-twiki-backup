---++Condor Hold Code 2

---+++Known causes:
Hold Code 2 means that Globus reported an error.  The subcode is the Globus Error code.

=Code 2 Subcode &lt;GRAM error code&gt;=

---+++Recommendations:

Attempt to resolve the Globus error and release the job using:
<pre class="screen">
%UCL_PROMPT_SHORT% condor_release &lt;job_id&gt;
</pre>

---+++See also

[[Documentation.Release3.GlobusErrors][Globus Error Codes]]
