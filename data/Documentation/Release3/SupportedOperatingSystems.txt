---+ OSG Software 3 Supported Operating Systems

OSG Software 3.4 and 3.3 are supported on Red Hat Enterprise 6 and 7 and select rebuilds, on 64-bit (RHEL 6 and RHEL 7) and 32-bit (RHEL 6 only) Intel architectures.

Specifically, we support:

   * !CentOS 6
   * !CentOS 7
   * Red Hat Enterprise Linux 6
   * Red Hat Enterprise Linux 7
   * Scientific Linux 6
   * Scientific Linux 7

Historical note: OSG Software 3.1 and 3.2 were supported on Red Hat Enterprise Linux 5 and 6 and select rebuilds, on 64- and 32-bit Intel architectures.
