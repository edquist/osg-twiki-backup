---+ OSG Software 3 Supported Operating Systems
%DOC_STATUS_TABLE%

OSG Software 3.2 is supported on Red Hat Enterprise Linux 5 and 6 and select rebuilds, on 64- and 32-bit Intel architectures.

OSG Software 3.3 is supported on Red Hat Enterprise 6 and 7 and select rebuilds, on 64-bit (RHEL 6 and RHEL 7) and 32-bit (RHEL 6 only) Intel architectures.

Specifically, we support:

   * !CentOS 5 (3.2 only)
   * !CentOS 6 (3.2 and 3.3)
   * !CentOS 7 (3.3 only)
   * Red Hat Enterprise Linux 5 (3.2 only)
   * Red Hat Enterprise Linux 6 (3.2 and 3.3)
   * Red Hat Enterprise Linux 7 (3.3 only)
   * Scientific Linux 5 (3.2 only)
   * Scientific Linux 6 (3.2 and 3.3)
   * Scientific Linux 7 (3.3 only)

Historical note: OSG Software 3.1 was supported on Red Hat Enterprise Linux 5 and 6 and select rebuilds, on 64- and 32-bit Intel architectures.
