---++Condor Hold Code 13

---+++Syntax

=Code 13 subcode &lt;unix_errno&gt;=

---+++Known causes:

Hold Code 13 indicates that condor could failed to open the output file.

---+++Recommendations:

Check your firewall settings and see [[InstallCondor#NetworkingReq][Condor ports]].

Check file permissions
