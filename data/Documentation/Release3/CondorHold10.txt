---++Condor Hold Code 10

---+++Syntax

=Code 10 subcode &lt;unix_errno&gt;=

---+++Known causes:

Hold Code 10 indicates that condor could not open the standard input stream.

---+++Recommendations:

Check your firewall settings and see [[CondorFirewalls][Condor ports]].
