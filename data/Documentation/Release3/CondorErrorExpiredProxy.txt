---++Expired Proxy

---+++Syntax:

=ERROR: proxy has expired=

---+++Known causes:

Your proxy has expired  

---+++Recommendations:

   * Obtain a new proxy using:
<pre class="screen">
%UCL_PROMPT_SHORT% grid-proxy-init
</pre>
or
<pre class="screen">
%UCL_PROMPT_SHORT% voms-proxy-init
</pre>

Submit the job to the grid as the same user whose certificate was authenticated with grid-proxy-init.

i.e. %RED%*do not do*%ENDCOLOR%:
<pre class="screen">
%UCL_PROMPT_SHORT% grid-proxy-init
%UCL_PROMPT_SHORT% su
%UCL_PROMPT_SHORT% globus-job-run &lt;site&gt; &lt;job&gt;
</pre>
