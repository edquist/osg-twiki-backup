Each installation or procedure document in the OSG Documentation TWiki has an Tester.
%BR%
%BR%
The Tester of a document should have knowledge about the topic of the document, the facilities to test the procedure or installation, and is responsible for:
   1. Testing the instructions on the software and version the document is meant to be used with
   1. Working in collaboration with the Owner of the document to resolve issues from a test of procedures
   1. Notifying the Document Architect if unable to continue to fulfill the responsibilities of the Reviewer
