---+++To register as a new user, please do the following:

Send an email to osg-TWiki@opensciencegrid.org with the following information:


   * First & last name:
   * Email address:
   * Country: 
   * VO Sponsor: (If you don't know your VO Sponsor, please contact your [[http://www.grid.iu.edu/osg-includes/sc.php][VO Support Center]])


This email will notify someone to add you to the TWiki manually.  You will then be asked to set your password.  You should expect a response time of 2 business days.
