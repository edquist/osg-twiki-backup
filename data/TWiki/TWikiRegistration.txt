---+++To register as a new user, please do the following:

First, obtain an OIM account.  You can do this by going to [[http://oim.grid.iu.edu/][http://oim.grid.iu.edu/]] with your DOEGrids or other IGTF Certificate loaded into your browser.  There is a link to register on the page you will see.

Once you do that, or if you already have an OIM account, then please do the following:

Send an email to osg-TWiki@opensciencegrid.org with the following information:


   * First & last name:
   * Email address:
   * Country: 
   * VO Sponsor: (If you don't know your VO Sponsor, please contact your [[https://oim.grid.iu.edu/pub/sc/show.php][VO Support Center]])


This email will notify the GOC to add you to the TWiki manually.  After approval, you will be sent an email with a set of instructions to follow.  Once those instructions are complete, you should be able to edit the twiki. You should expect an initial response time of 2 business days.
