---++ Pre-Requisites for VORS

Your resource must be registered with the [[https://oim.grid.iu.edu][OSG Information Management]] (OIM) system and you must represent your resource at the [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Operations/ProductionMeetingMinutes][weekly operations meeting]] before you will appear in VORS.

---++ VORS Location

VORS is located [[http://vors.grid.iu.edu][here]]. You will be added to VORS when you register your site at the GOC and attend the Operations call to represent your site.

---++ Explanation of VORS Methods

VORS uses the site_verify.pl, located locally at $VDT_LOCATION/verify/ to run site level testing using the MIS VO. Failure (Red) indicates one of the following conditions. 
   * Failure to Authenticate (Check globus-gatekeeper.log at $VDT_LOCATION/globus/var/globus-gatekeeper.log for hints)
   * Failure to Run a Fork Job (Check globus-gatekeeper.log at $VDT_LOCATION/globus/var/globus-gatekeeper.log for hints)
   * Failure to Find a Job Manager (Check Job Manager specific log files)
   * Failure to Transfer files with GSIFTP correctly (Check $VDT_LOCATION/globus/var/gridftp.log)

---++ VORS Documentation

   * Review [[http://vors.grid.iu.edu/documentation/index.html][VORS Documentation]] for help

---++ Successful VORS Update

   * If your site passes you will see %CACHE% %VERSION%  in the Grid Column of VORS.
