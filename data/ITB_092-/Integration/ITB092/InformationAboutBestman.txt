---++ Introduction
!BeStMan is an LBNL implementation of SRM based on Unix disks and HPSS. It has been ported to NCAR MSS. SRM/L-Store from Vanderbilt University uses !BeStMan as its front-end SRM and L-Store as its back-end MSS.

Information can be found at [[http://datagrid.lbl.gov/bestman/bestman-manual.html][Berkeley Storage Manager (!BeStMan)]].
