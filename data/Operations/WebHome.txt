---++Introduction


---+++!!Operations Activity Objectives and Scope


This OSG Activity is charged with preparation and deployment
of the infrastructure used for the production Operations,
including the documentation that describes how the various
entities (users, VOs, resource providers, other grids, etc.)
interact and participate with OSG Operations.
The [[SupportCenters.WebHome][Support Centers Technical Group]] provides the primary
oversight and guidance to this activity including maintaining
the model and roadmap for OSG Operations.
