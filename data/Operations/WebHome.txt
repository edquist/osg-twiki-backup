---++Introduction


---+++!!Operations Activity Objectives and Scope

This OSG Activity is charged with preparation and deployment
of the infrastructure used for the production Operations,
including the documentation that describes how the various
entities (users, VOs, resource providers, other grids, etc.)
interact and participate with OSG Operations.
The Support Centers Technical Group provides the primary
oversight and guidance to this activity including maintaining
the model and roadmap for OSG Operations. 

%SILVER%
Old Statement:<br/>
This OSG Activity is involved with running the grid-wide services including provisioning and installation of middleware and operational support for those services, resource providers, and VO's running on OSG. All the support centers hosting services and providing VO and end-user support for OSG are participants in this activity. 
%ENDCOLOR%
