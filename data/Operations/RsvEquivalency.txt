---+ RSV WLCG SAM Equivalences

This page expands on availability (a.k.a critical) metrics for various OSG services in the WLCG Sam/GridView availability calculations. The SAM team maintains [[https://twiki.cern.ch/twiki/bin/view/LCG/OSGCriticalProbes][a similar page for their reference]]. This list is used by the [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Operations/RsvSAMGridView][SAM/GridView system]], the [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/MeasurementsAndMetrics/RsvReportsOverview][RSV reporting system]] as well as [[http://myosg.grid.iu.edu/resources][the central RSV status interface]].
