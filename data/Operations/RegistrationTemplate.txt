<p>Notes:</p>
<ol>
  <li><a name="Note1"/> Operations Contact – the person or
group who will be the normal day to day contact point for operational
issues. For most large organizations this is a group with an email list
that distributes to several people and a phone number that is directed
by rotation to different people. For small organization it might be an
individual person. This is the address to which OSG trouble tickets will be routed. </li>
  <li><a name="Note2"/>Security Contact –
the person who can provide information about the security procedures
for the resource or service being registered, and answer the question
about what names, email addresses or phone numbers should be included
in the Incident Response contact database. Providing the incident
response contact information is a separate process from this
registration form.  Only required for services that store delegated credentials.  Enter N/A if this does not apply.</li>
  <li><a name="Note3"/>VO | Resource |
Service Name – an option to allow a VO or resource or service name to
be different than the organization name responsible for it. If contact
information is different for the VO|resource|service than the
organization then separate complete registration forms need to be
filled out for each.</li>
  <li><a name="Note4"/>Resource | Service
URL – a URL pointing to a description of the resources and/or services
provided by the organization. This may be a simple web page maintained
by the organization or a URL to some type of discovery service. It is
NOT intended to be a URL pointing to each individual grid service such
as GRAM, !GridFTP, MDS, etc. but rather points to something which
displays the aggregated set of services provided by this organization.</li>
</ol>
