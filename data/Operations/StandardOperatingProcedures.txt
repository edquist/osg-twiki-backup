---+ Standard Operating Procedures for OSG Operations.

Operating procedures are initiated by some type of event that causes or motivates
an action to occur.  The main purpose of the documents are to guide the human
actions an illustrate the workflow.  The procedures below have been approved by the
Support Centers Technical Group. Draft procedures under development are at
Operations.DevelopingStandardOperatingProcedures.

---++ Requirements

Procedures for GOC and Support Center staff contributing to OSG Operations
need to be documented, aproved, published, and followed, to make a smooth and efficient
operation for OSG, and to enable a process for improving operations.

	* Publication point for procedures
	* Type of access control for SOP documents
