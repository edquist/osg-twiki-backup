---+ Root Cause Analysis of Reported Sno+ Issue

---++ Introduction
This page will explain in detail the events that led up to the failure to update the CRLs in OASIS on Wednesday, September 2, 2015. This led to CRLs not being up to date and created the potential to cause job failure at resources. 

At that time a ticket was opened at https://ticket.opensciencegrid.org/26674 reporting the issue.  

---++ Timeline

   * September 2 - Initial ticket opened at https://ticket.opensciencegrid.org/26674
   * 5/Sep, 14:53 (eastern) Scott contacted via text message.
   * ~15:00, CRLs manually updated, update propagated, verified on GOC worker node and xd-login
   * 6-7/Sep, CRL manual updates

---++ Analysis

   * Problem persists as of this writing
