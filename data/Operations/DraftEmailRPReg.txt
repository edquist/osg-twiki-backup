Draft email acknowledgement to resource registration:

"Thank you for registering the [RP_Name] Resource. Your registration  will be added to the OSG Resource Structure.

As a registered OSG resource you should have a representative attend  the weekly OSG Operations Meeting to review issues facing your site  and the OSG community.

There are several mailing lists associated with the OSG, as a new  Resource you may want to subscribe to the following mailing lists:

osg@opensciencegrid.org  -- For general OSG announcements
osg-general@opensciencegrid.org  -- For general technical discussion  and open community support
osg-operaions@opensciencegrid.org  -- Operations activities
osg-ops-status@opensciencegrid.org  -- OSG Status Reports

Details for subscribing to these lists and a list of other OSG  mailing lists you may be interested in subscribing to can be found at  http://www.opensciencegrid.org/index.php? option=com_content&task=view&id=75&Itemid=88.

Please contact the Grid Operations Center (GOC) at  goc@opensciencegrid.org if you have any questions.

Thank you for registering your resource, and we look forward to your  participation in the OSG!

OSG Operations Workgroup"
