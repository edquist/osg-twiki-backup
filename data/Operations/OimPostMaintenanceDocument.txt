---+ Post Upgrade Functionality Checks

This document is to provide a list of checks that will test the functionality of the OIM service after an upgrade has been done.

---++ Checks Immediately after service is brought up
   * Execute /opt/service-monitor/oim/test.sh Any errors will generate an alert e-mail
   * Verify the [[https://oim.grid.iu.edu/oim/home][OIM]] web page is up. Page should load in a reasonable amount of time and contain the expected contact.
   * Attempt to update your contact information for example, [[https://oim.grid.iu.edu/oim/contactedit?id=652][here]]. Click on "contacts",  "show detail" under your name then on "edit". Click "Update confirmation date" and "submit".  Edit it again to verify the date has changed.
