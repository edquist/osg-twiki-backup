---+ OASIS Status Update

---++ Introduction

The OASIS service uses CVMFS and Squid caching to distribute application software to OSG resources. The follow is a list of VOs and Resources that have enabled OASIS. These listings will be updated weekly on the first business day of the week until OASIS has widespread adoption on the OSG

---++ VOs Using OASIS
   * CSIU (via OSG)
   * gm2
   * HCC
   * !IceCube
   * !NoVA
   * SBGrid
   * UC3

---++ OSG Sites That Have Enabled OASIS on Worker Nodes
   * Nebraska
   * SMU
   * Wisconsin
