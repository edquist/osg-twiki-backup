---+ Latest Draft Documents

Von's feedback on the latest drafts:

 In general, I'm concerned about the level of legality we're putting on the RA Agents (and Sponsors?). Sociologically this is going to be a big jump for them from the current agreements (see A.3 of http://www.doegrids.org/Docs/CP-CPS.pdf). A specific concern is by requiring indemnification, this will change the document from something they sign personally, into a institutionally signed document, which will bring a whole mess of lawyers into this process. I'd like to discuss and see what flexibility we have at this level.

 I'm confused about Sponsor registration - the structure map shows a "Sponsor Agmt" but I don't believe I've seen this document. I do have an Sponsor Attestation document, but I understand that to be per-issuance. (I thought we discussed at one time Sponsorships was something RA Agents would simply record?)

 I think there is still some language in 6.2 of the RPS that assumes a crypographic module is in use, which will not be the case for OSG. I'll send some proposed minor changes in a separate email.

 And I'd like some pricing information on what happens if the certificate levels described in 5.1 of the Master Agreement are exceeded.
