---+ Links to RSV, SAM and !GridView Summaries

---++ RSV

   * The GOC produces [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/MeasurementsAndMetrics/RsvReportsOverview][daily email reports for Availability/Reliability]] using a [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Operations/RsvEquivalency#Critical_Tests_for_OSG_Resources][list of availability metrics]].
   * MyOSG is the official place to look for [[http://myosg.grid.iu.edu/wizard?all_resources=on&gridtype=on&gridtype_1=on&summary_attrs_showservice=on&summary_attrs_showrsvstatus=on&summary_attrs_showfqdn=on&gip_status_attrs_showfqdn=on&gip_status_attrs_showtestresults=on][web presentation]] of RSV metric results.

---++ RSV to SAM/GridView Transport (Metric Results and Downtime Records)

<p> *RSV metric results* received by the central collector at the GOC as well as *downtime records* from the OIM system are uploaded to the WLCG SAM if a resource-service is listed with interop_monitoring=true on [[http://myosg.grid.iu.edu/rgsummary/index?datasource=summary&summary_attrs_showwlcg=on&summary_attrs_showservice=on&summary_attrs_showfqdn=on&gip_status_attrs_showtestresults=on&gip_status_attrs_showfqdn=on&account_type=cumulative_hours&ce_account_type=gip_vo&se_account_type=vo_transfer_volume&start_type=7daysago&start_date=03/20/2009&end_type=now&end_date=03/27/2009&all_resources=on&gridtype=on&gridtype_1=on&service_4=on&service_1=on&service_5=on&service_2=on&service_3=on&active=on&active_value=1&disable_value=1][MyOSG resource summary]] ([[http://myosg.grid.iu.edu/rgsummary/xml?datasource=summary&summary_attrs_showwlcg=on&summary_attrs_showservice=on&summary_attrs_showfqdn=on&gip_status_attrs_showtestresults=on&gip_status_attrs_showfqdn=on&account_type=cumulative_hours&ce_account_type=gip_vo&se_account_type=vo_transfer_volume&start_type=7daysago&start_date=03/20/2009&end_type=now&end_date=03/27/2009&all_resources=on&gridtype=on&gridtype_1=on&service_4=on&service_1=on&service_5=on&service_2=on&service_3=on&active=on&active_value=1&disable_value=1][XML Equivalent]]). The format of the above listing is as follows: 
<pre>&lt;ResourceSummary&gt;
 &lt;ResourceGroup&gt;
  &lt;GridType&gt;OSG Production Resource&lt;/GridType&gt;
      . . . 
  &lt;GroupName&gt;AGLT2&lt;/GroupName&gt;
  &lt;Resources&gt;
   &lt;Resource&gt;
      . . . 
    &lt;Name&gt;AGLT2&lt;/Name&gt;
      . . . 
    &lt;Services&gt;
     &lt;Service&gt;
      . . . 
      &lt;Name&gt;CE&lt;/Name&gt;
      . . . 
     &lt;/Service&gt;
    &lt;/Services&gt;
    &lt;FQDN&gt;gate01.aglt2.org&lt;/FQDN&gt;
    &lt;WLCGInformation&gt;
      . . . 
     &lt;InteropMonitoring&gt;True&lt;/InteropMonitoring&gt;
      . . . 
    &lt;/WLCGInformation&gt;
   &lt;/Resource&gt;
       . . . 
&lt;/ResourceGroup&gt;
  . . . 
&lt;/ResourceSummary&gt;</pre> 

<p> Service can be =CE=, =GridFtp=, =SRMv1=, =SRMv2=. 
   * Note that the above information is dynamically generated using Interop Monitoring flag setting for each resource in OIM. 
   * In OIM, anyone who is listed as an admin or security or misc contact for a resource can edit that resource to check/uncheck the flag 
   * If you think you should be able to modify a resource but do not have access, [[https://ticker.grid.iu.edu/goc][contact the GOC]]. 

---+++ Data Format
   * The RSV records are uploaded in the format agreed up on, in the [[https://twiki.cern.ch/twiki/bin/view/LCG/GridMonitoringProbeSpecification][WLCG Grid Monitoring Probes Specification]].
   * The downtime record are uploaded in the format described in [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/pub/Operations/RsvSAMGridView/2008-05-WLCG_OSG_Downtime_Specification-1.1.pdf][this specifications document]].
