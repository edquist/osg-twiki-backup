---++ About This Document
This document provides quick links for RSV (Resource and Service Validation), SAM (Service Availability Monitoring) and !GridView to show the availabiliry of OSG resources.

---++ RSV
   * The GOC produces [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Trash/Trash/Trash/Trash/MeasurementsAndMetrics/RsvReportsOverview][daily email reports for Availability/Reliability]] using a [[https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Operations/RsvEquivalency#Critical_Tests_for_OSG_Resources][list of availability metrics]]. These reports are archived online [[http://rsv.grid.iu.edu/daily-reports/][here]].
   * !MyOSG is the official place to look for [[http://tinyurl.com/yfoygny][web presentation]] of RSV metric results.

---++ SAM

NOTE
   1 Ensure you have a DOEGrids issued X509 certificate loaded into your web browser. 
   1 In each of the following URLs, <u>ensure that all the metrics you would like to view are selected</u> in the *Tests Displayed* list box on the top left corner 
   1 To view results for only the critical metric (i.e the ones that are used in availability calculations), click on the *show ops critical tests* check box. 

   * [[https://lcg-sam.cern.ch:8443/sam/sam.py?sensors=OSGCE&regions=OpenScienceGrid&vo=ops&order=SiteName&funct=ShowSensorTests][Summary of OSG CEs in SAM]]
